When to Expect Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 254 Spoilers &amp; Leaks

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, August 4, 2024

When are the Jujutsu Kaisen chapter 254 spoilers and leaks going to be available? Fans are slowly losing hope since Sukuna is still standing strong. Here is when to expect the Jujutsu Kaisen chapter 254 manga plot leaks.

When to Expect Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 254 Spoilers

Fans can expect the Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 253 spoilers and leaks to be released by Thursday, March 21.

For those who are in the United States, the spoilers should be available around Wednesday evening or past midnight (Thursday). That would be around lunchtime or early afternoon for those located in Asia. This is an estimate since the spoilers’ release time may change.

According to the chapter 253 spoilers, the series will be on break next week.

Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 253 will be out in the United States on Sunday, March 10, at 10 AM ET / 9 AM CT / 7 AM PT.

Meanwhile, the expected official release date for Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 254 is Sunday, March 24.

Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 253 Spoilers Recap

Some of the Jujutsu High adults are talking about the strongest grade 1 sorcerer. Mei Mei, Gojo, and Nanami think it’s Kusakabe. Kusakabe mentions it is someone other than him or Usami.

Maki vs. Sukuna continues. Sukuna uses a falling car to his advantage to attack Maki. After using Cleave on Maki, Ino appears and tries to attack. Sukuna uses his slashes to counterattack, and he realizes Ino isn’t holding Nanami’s weapon. Kusakabe appears and attacks Sukuna with Nanami’s technique, but he realizes Sukuna predicted that.

Maki thinks despite Yuta’s survival, dragging the fight against Sukuna any longer will result in more problems for them. The narration states that after Gojo’s death, Kashimo, Higuruma, and Yuta entertained Sukuna because of their talents. But Sukuna was excited to fight Maki because of her Heavenly Restriction.

He grabs Maki’s face while commending her and says her existence goes against Jujutsu. He uses Black Flash for the first time on Maki. Kusakabe panics after realizing he’s the last one standing and wonders if he can actually kill Sukuna.
