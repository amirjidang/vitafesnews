Name Sara Gore

Birthday / Age / Date of Birth / How old / DOB May 25, 1976. As of 2020, she is around 44 years old.


Biography Profile / Wikipedia Information

For NBCUniversal and LX Networks, Sara Gore is an Emmy winning lifestyle presenter. The plays, New York Live and the Open House, was presented by her.

She also presented the NBC Golden Globes Red Carpet Specials, the Primetime Emmy Awards, and the SNL 40th Anniversary Special.



Personal Life & Family Background :

Sara Gore was born to George and Gail Gore’s parents in Queensland, New York, in May 1976. She has two sisters who are older. Her parents are currently divorced despite having been together for 36 years.
Sara entered the High School of Queensbury and graduated in 1994. She proceeded to attend Marymount Manhattan School and graduated with a minor in Business/Theatre in Communications.


Gore is now married to a producer and screenwriter named Matthew Miele. Together, they have three daughters. Griffin, Sawyer and Fiona are 8, 6 and 3 years of age, respectively (ages as of 2020).
Sara has had a pet, called Mugs, even before her first child was born. She rescued a dog out of a sanctuary a number of years back and called her Cookie.
In her spare time, she volunteers locally for ‘City Harvest’ and for the Mayor’s Animals Partnership for NYC.

Career, Income, Salary & Net Worth :

By serving as a personal chef, Sara began her career with a passion for cooking, running her own catering business.
Later on, she served as a line cook for Jean-Georges Vongerichten, the globally acclaimed chef and restaurateur.


She quit her work to do an off Broadway play as a line chef, and during that time she encountered a hosting agent who began applying her for hosting jobs related to food.
She also did a Food Network pilot that never got off the ground and a General Mills culinary series afterwards.
Her success was when she got a position with the Code TV start-up lifestyle group, which later changed its name to LXT.
