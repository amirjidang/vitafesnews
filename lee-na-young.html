Who is South Korean actress Lee Na Young?

Lee Na-young is a South Korean actress. She is known for her lead roles in the TV Series ‘Ruler of Your Own World’, released in 2002, and the film Someone Special. Aside from acting in many TV shows and movies, Lee is known for appearing in numerous commercials.

Biography Timeline

Lee Na Young is a South Korean actress and model. She was born on February 22, 1979, in Seoul, South Korea. Here is a detailed timeline of her life and career.

  • February 22, 1979: Lee Na Young was born in Seoul, South Korea.
  • 1997: Lee Na Young debuts as a model in a commercial for Lotte Samkang.
  • 1999: Lee Na Young’s acting debut in the TV series “Ruler of Your Own World.”
  • 2003: Lee Na Young stars in the film “Please Teach Me English” as the character of Young-Joo.
  • 2005: Lee Na Young appears in the film “Who’s Got the Tape?” as the character of Soo-jin.
  • 2008: Lee Na Young stars in the film “Dream” as the character of Park So-yeon.
  • 2014: Lee Na Young marries actor Won Bin.
  • 2015: Lee Na Young gives birth to a son.
  • 2021: Lee Na Young stars in the TV series “Mine” as the character of Seo Hee-soo.

Lee Na Young’s talent and beauty have made her one of the most beloved actresses in South Korea. Her performances in films and TV series have won her critical acclaim, and her work as a model has made her a fashion icon. Despite her success, Lee Na Young has maintained a low profile and focused on her family life. Fans admire her for her talent, grace, and dedication to her craft.

Personal Life

Lee is married to actor Won Bin. They tied the knot on May 30 2015. The couple also has a child. Their only child was born in December 2015. Career

Lee’s career started as a model in 1998. Working on the supporting roles in television dramas Did We Love?, KAIST, and Queen, Lee starred in the tepidly received 2001 Hong Kong sci-fi action film Dream of A Warrior starring Leon Lai. She also appeared in the 2001 music video for “Catherine’s Wheel” by Britpop band Rialto.

Lee has covered everything from films to modelling for brands to TV shows and music videos. Since beginning her career, she has won many hearts and awards. In 2002, she won the Excellence Award, Actress in a Miniseries, for her work’ Ruler of Your Own World’. The same year, she was nominated as Best Actress (TV). Gaining appreciation from the beginning, she has paved her path to this date. Lee’s latest film, also out in 2018, where she played the role of a mother (Beautiful Days).

Her latest TV series was released in 2019, ‘ Romance Is a Bonus Book’. In the series, she portrayed the role of Kang Dan-Yi. From 1998 till 2019, she has been a part of many films and TV series. The audience loved her for her skills and appearance on screen.
