Justin Bieber Is Allegedly Struggling After Hailey Insists On Taking Time Apart Amid Divorce Rumors

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Highlights

  • Hailey Bieber is reportedly uncertain about her marriage with Justin, causing him mental health issues.
  • Justin bought a separate mansion to give Hailey space, but still struggles with their separation.
  • Both Justin and Hailey are trying to save their marriage, despite rumors and concerns about Justin's mental health.

Though fans have accused Hailey Bieber of doing her best to distract from the divorce rumors surrounding her and Justin’s marriage, a new report suggests she’s the one who’s unsure of where they stand, and it’s apparently affecting Justin’s mental health.

Speaking to National Enquirer, a source explained that the reason Justin purchased a new $16 million mansion recently was to give his wife space, after she apparently requested they take some time apart to sort through their relationship issues.

Some Fans Believe Justin And Hailey Are Living Apart

"Justin got another place in Beverly Hills so he could give Hailey the space she wanted," an insider said. "He's only there a couple of nights a week, if that, but it's still really tough on him.”

"He's so dependent on Hailey," they continued, insisting Justin is trying to save his marriage while Hailey may be the reluctant one.. "She's everything to him — personal cheerleader, assistant, cook, muse. He's lost without her."

They added, "But he's trying to be tough about it because this is something Hailey wants!"

This echoes earlier reports that suggest Justin was staying at a Beverly Hills hotel, despite owning property nearby with his wife, after he was pictured by photographers in the morning, prompting speculation the couple have been living apart.

Justin’s Health Is Allegedly Suffering Amid Marital Problems

Previous reports have said those close to Justin are worried about his mental health, especially after he shared a crying selfie with no context, which caused concern online amongst his fans.

"Justin has been facing some difficulties lately. He has been having a hard time and hasn't been feeling like his usual self," a source told Entertainment Tonight.

Nevertheless, the insider emphasized that Hailey has been supportive of Justin. "Hailey has been doing her best to be there for Justin, but it is upsetting for her to see him struggle," they went on. "They are doing their best to make things work and be supportive of each other. They are committed to doing whatever it takes to make things better."

The news broke in April that Justin had brought property, without it being clear if the purchase was a joint one with his wife. But given divorce rumors had been circulating for months at that point, many fans took it as more evidence that something is amiss in the couple’s marriage.

Many people became further convinced of problems when Hailey’s dad Stephen Baldwin made a plea on social media for prayers for the couple, without providing further context. Hailey subsequently released a vague statement that seemed to call out the speculation, asking her followers not to believe the rumors.

But with Hailey and Justin hesitant to comment on the growing speculation, and Justin’s crying selfie fresh in the mind of his followers, it doesn’t seem like the buzz about this couple will die down naturally.
