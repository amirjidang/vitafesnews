What Did Courtney Vucekovich Accuse Armie Hammer Of?

On January 14th of 2021, Page Six published an article looking at the accusations that Armie Hammer’s ex-girlfriend Courtney Vucekovich made against the actor. According to Vucekovich's most headline-grabbing comments, Hammer spoke about wanting to consume part of her.

“He said to me he wants to break my rib and barbecue and eat it.” While that obviously is a strange thing for anyone to say, if Armie Hammer did make that statement, he could have just been joking around.

On top of her claim about Armie Hammer making that statement, Courtney Vucekovich claimed that he would react in a strange way when she got cut. “He says, ‘I want to take a bite out of you.’ If I had a little cut on my hand, he’d like suck it or lick it. That’s about as weird as we got.”

After Courtney Vucekovich’s accusations came to light, Armie Hammer put out a statement to multiple sources that started tellingly. "I'm not responding to these bulls**t claims”. Later in the same statement, Hammer called the claims against him “vicious and spurious online attacks”.

Courtney Vucekovich Addressed The Bite Mark Photo Controversy

When the world first learned that Armie Hammer had been accused of some disturbing behavior, a lot of observers didn’t look into the story any further than reading the headlines. As a result, it makes perfect sense that a documentary that delved deeper into the scandal than that was produced.

Related: Armie Hammer Given The Side Eye After Reports He's 'Thriving' In Rehab

During the production of that documentary, House of Hammer, Courtney Vucekovich was interviewed and given the chance to tell her story. When the documentary premiered, Vucekovich’s accusations against Hammer were reexamined and some viewers pointed out details about her story that didn’t add up in their opinion.

Most notably, viewers took note of a photo that purportedly showed a bite mark on Courtney Vucekovich’s arm that came from Armie Hammer. However, some viewers researched the image and discovered that the photo in question actually was of a tattoo a completely uninvolved person had.

Once the true origin of the bite mark photo came to light, Courtney Vucekovich put out a statement about the revelation.

“When you are love-bombed, you receive multiple images in rapid succession. During my time with Armie, I received numerous messages, including countless images and videos.”

“The bite mark shown was a photo sent by Armie within our archived text thread and over a year later, I believed it to have been a photo of me given that I have dozens of photos depicting his abuse on my body.”

What Is Armie Hammer’s Accuser Courtney Vucekovich Up To Now?

Even though a lot of people only know Courtney Vucekovich’s name due to her involvement with Armie Hammer, she is her own person outside of the scandal. With that in mind, it shouldn’t surprise anyone to see that Vucekovich’s current life seems pretty incredible from afar.

Thanks to Courtney Vucekovich’s Instagram page, it is known that she is the founder and CEO of a company called FLASHD. Focused on the FLASHD app, Vucekovich’s company allows customers in the Dallas-Fort Worth area to use their phones to book a team of beauty professionals.

Once an appointment is made, a “personal glam squad” will travel to wherever the customer is. Upon arrival, the team of beauty professionals will do the customer’s makeup and hair. Finally, FLASHD’s team of professionals will then take beautiful photos of the customer.

On top of being a business owner and CEO, Courtney Vucekovich's Instagram page depicts her life as glamorous and full of travel. Since Vucekovich’s accusations about Armie Hammer came to light in January of 2021, Courtney has posted many photos of herself in gorgeous locations on Instagram.
