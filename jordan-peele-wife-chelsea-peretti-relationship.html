Who Is Jordan Peele Married To?

Thanks to the success that Chelsea Peretti And Jordan Peele have both enjoyed during their careers, both have millions of fans who would love to spend time with them. However, the two stars seem to be each other's biggest fans as they have reportedly loved being together for years now.

In this day and age, a lot of celebrity couples seem to document every moment they are together on social media. On the other hand, Jordan Peele and Chelsea Peretti have mostly kept their relationship private.

For proof of how much Jordan Peele and Chelsea Peretti like to keep their relationship to themselves, all you have to do is keep in mind the couple eloped. Since both performers are highly successful, they certainly could have afforded to throw a ceremony and reception.

After Chelsea Peretti and Jordan Peele tied the knot in 2016, they welcomed their son Beaumont into the world in 2017. From all accounts, the family has been happy together ever since.

Chelsea Peretti's Quote About Her Husband Revealed A Sad Truth

Over the years, there have been a few Hollywood couples who truly seemed to be on equal footing in the business. A perfect example of that was Demi Moore and Bruce Willis back when they were married.

During Chelsea Peretti and Ike Barinholtz's interview, both of them were asked to explain when they met Key and Peele. After Peretti explained that she met Peele after he reached out on social media to praise a project she had worked on, she asked a telling question.

Since Chelsea Peretti was afraid to speak openly about her Brooklyn Nine-Nine exit, the fact that she was unsure about whether to be open about her relationship with Jordan Peele arguably says a lot.

Of course, Peretti is known for her sense of humor so she may have just been mocking the idea of explaining how she met Peele like they weren't in love at that time.

Right after Chelsea Perretti and Jordan Peele got married, she spoke about her husband briefly during talk show appearances but she typically avoids talking about Peele except in general terms.

With that in mind, there is a very good chance that Chelsea Peretti avoids talking about her husband because she worries being compared to Jordan Peele could damage her career. After all, the sad truth of the matter is that sexism often results in women in power couples being treated differently.
