Inside Barron Hilton II And His Wife's Insanely Impressive Net Worths

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Highlights

  • Barron Hilton II leads a private life and has a net worth of around $5 million. He owns Hilton Estates Group and Casa Del Sol tequila brand.
  • Barron and Tessa met in St. Barts in 2016 and got married in the same location two years later. Tessa comes from a wealthy background and is the CEO and Co-Founder of Hilton Estates Group. They have two children and are expecting their third.
  • Tessa Hilton is worth $5 million and is related to King Charles III through her father. She is the CEO and Co-Founder of Hilton Estates Group and also produces her own music.

Hotels, reality television, the music world, and even the Metaverse have been infiltrated by the Hilton family. As the world has gotten to know the dynamic and ever-successful family, the last name has become synonymous with the word rich. Even with Barron Hilton donating 97% of the Hilton's money to charity after his death in 2019, he left the remaining 3% to his family.

That money is thought to have been split evenly among his eight children, 15 grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren. While we know a lot about what Paris and Nicky Hilton have been doing with their money, their brother Barron Hilton II has been far less forthright with his life.

Here's a look at his net worth and that of his wife.

Who Is Barron Hilton II?

Barron Hilton II is the grandson of billionaire, Baron Hilton

Barron has led a much more private life than his mother and sisters. The 32-year-old spent part of his childhood living with his siblings and parents in the Waldorf Astoria Hotel. Kathy Hilton shared during the Real Housewives of Beverly HillsReunion that the family spent nine years living there together, and it was some of her favorite memories with the bunch.

"It was very, very lovely," Kathy said. "We loved being in close quarters under one roof, except when Paris would sneak out of the service elevator."

While Barron may keep mum on some things, one thing that he is vocal about is how much he loves his wife, Tessa.

How Barron Hilton And Countess Tessa June Von Walderdorff Met

Barron Hilton and Tessa Hilton met at St. Barts

Barron met his future wife, Countess Tessa June von Walderdorff, in 2016 while vacationing in St. Barts. The island is a favorite of both the Hiltons and von Walderdorffs and the two developed a special bond during their time together there.

Tessa told Spirit & Flesh magazine that the pair's connection was instant.

"Laying eyes on Barron for the first time was so surreal, almost like a dream," Tessa explained. "He was sitting there, next to me, and it was as if someone had all of a sudden pressed 'mute' on the remote control to silence the movie around us."

Two years after meeting, Barron and Tessa got engaged. They decided to get married in the same location they met, having an intimate wedding in St. Barts with friends and family.

Tessa's parents got married at the same spot 30 years earlier, making the event even more meaningful for the couple.

"It has always been my happy place," Tessa explained to Brides. "I would have never imagined that one day the island would introduce me to the love of my life."

What Does Barron Hilton II Do?

Barron Hilton owns Hilton Estates Group and Casa Del Sol

Barron currently owns Hilton Estates Group and Casa Del Sol tequila brand. He and Tessa throw lavish parties to help boost the appeal of luxury homes and sell homes as well. Barron has a net worth of around $5 million.

The real estate mogul is also a devoted father. He and Tessa welcomed their first child in March 2020, a daughter named Milou Alizée Hilton.

"We found the name Milou from where we met, Pointe Milou in St Barts. We thought it was only fitting," the couple explained to People while sharing her birth announcement.

In an Instagram post, Tessa told followers that motherhood has been the most enchanting gift she could have asked for.

The couple welcomed their second child, a boy named Caspian Barron Hilton, in September 2022. They confirmed that Caspian was a surprise, but a much-welcomed one at that.

Tessa was pregnant simultaneously with Barron's eldest sister, Nicky. The two women loved being able to share the experience together, sharing pictures of their matching bumps throughout their pregnancies.

As of September 2023, the couple shared that they are expecting their third baby together.

How Much Is Barron Hilton II's Wife, Countess Tessa June von Walderdorff, Worth?

Tessa Hilton is worth $5 million

Now known by her married name, Tessa Hilton comes from a wealthy background. Her father is photographer and artist Count Franz von Walderdorff, and her mother is artist Anna-Sabrina Brühwiler. The royal family she is a descendant of ruled over the Kingdom of Bavaria. She's related to King Charles III through her father, as well.

French was Tessa's first language, later learning German and English.

According to Tessa's Instagram profile, she's the CEO and Co-Founder of Hilton Estates Group. She also produces her own music, dabbling in DJing after finding a love for the art in her late teens and 20s. Surely she and her sister-in-law Paris Hilton have plenty to talk about as the heiress has been influential in the scene, working in Ibiza for five years.

Online sources report that she has the same net worth as her husband, coming in at $5 million.
