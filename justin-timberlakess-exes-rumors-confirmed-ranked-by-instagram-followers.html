Justin Timberlakes's Exes (Rumors &amp; Confirmed) Ranked By Instagram Followers

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, August 12, 2024

Justin Timberlake is currently married to Jessica Biel and they seem to be very happy together. That doesn’t mean that he hasn’t done his fair share of dating in the past before settling down! Justin Timberlake has a long list of famous ex-girlfriends and all of them share a few mutual qualities in common from talent to beauty.

Something else a lot of the women share in common? A lot of Instagram followers! Here is how Justin Timberlake's ex-girlfriends, ex-flings, and rumored romantic partners rank in terms of popularity on the most popular social media app ever – Instagram

10 Mila Kunis (Rumor) - No Instagram

When Justin Timberlake and Mila Kunis filmed the movie Friends with Benefits together fans everywhere thought they had something real going on behind the scenes. With their perfectly amazing onscreen chemistry, movie-watchers everywhere assumed that the two were really dating, in real life. Justin and Mila both never confirmed anything and now she is with Ashton Kutcher. As of today, she doesn't have an Instagram account at all.

9 Olivia Munn (Rumor) - 2.7 Million Followers

Olivia Munn has 2.7 million followers on the gram these days. In 2010, rumors about her and Justin Timberlake started to spread. Justin Timberlake was already in a relationship with Jessica Biel by this time so people were speculating that he was having an affair. The reason why? They were spotted being flirty at a Myspace event that year. They both never confirmed anything.

8 Alyssa Milano - 2.9 Million Followers

Justin Timberlake officially dated Alyssa Milano in 2002 but the relationship only lasted for a brief few months. This short-lived romance came to be after he split from Britney Spears. Alyssa Milano is now married to David Bugliari and she has been since 2009. They share a baby together as well. Back in 2002, Justin and Alyssa were spotted going to nightclubs and golf courses together.

7 Fergie - 4.8 Million Followers

These days, Fergie has 4.8 million followers on Instagram. These are fans who care to keep up with her in her day-to-day life. Back in the late 90s, she was in an official relationship with Justin Timberlake. According to Celebuzz, Fergie said, “He was 16 and I was 23. It was before he got real heavy with Britney.” That is quite the age gap! Obviously, things between them did not work out since they have both moved on.

6 Jenna Dewan - 6.3 Million Followers

Jenna Dewan currently has 6.3 million followers on Instagram. Her most famous relationship in history would have to be her relationship with Channing Tatum. Although her and Channing Tatum are no longer together, they met on the set of step up and seemed to be really happy together for several years! Before Jenna Dewan was with Channing Tatum, she dated Justin Timberlake in 2002 after working as his back up dancer.

5 Cameron Diaz - 7.6 Million Followers

Justin Timberlake and Cameron Diaz dated for four years! That is a long time… They called it quits in 2007 after several rumors made their rounds through the media about bouts of jealousy and a lack of trust between the two celebs.

There are rumors that Justin Timberlake actually fell in love with Jessica Biel while he was still dating Cameron Diaz! No one knows for sure. As of right now, Cameron Diaz has 7.6 million followers on Instagram.

4 Lindsay Lohan - 8.7 Million Followers

Lindsay Lohan has 8.7 million followers on Instagram who are trying to keep up with everything she’s got going on in her life from brands that she is partnering with to nightclubs that she is potentially opening. She revealed on Watch What Happens Live that she hooked up with Justin Timberlake. She also revealed that she hooked up with James Franco, Adam Levine, Max George, Zac Efron, Colin Farrell Ashton Kutcher, Orlando Bloom, and several other Hollywood celebs.

3 Jessica Biel 9.3 Million Followers

Jessica Biel is Justin Timberlake‘s wife and they have been married since 2012. They had a one-of-a-kind Hollywood wedding and have now become one of the Hollywood power couples that other couples wish they could emulate.

They have gone through their fair share of highs and lows with rumors of infidelity even spreading through the media but at the end of the day, they are still totally connected and in love. They share two children together! These days, Jessica Biel has 9.3 million followers on Instagram.

2 Britney Spears - 27.3 Million Followers

Justin Timberlake’s ex-girlfriend who comes in second place for having the most followers on Instagram is Britney Spears who has 27.3 million followers as of right now. The relationship between Britney Spears and Justin Timberlake is one that will go down in history and never be forgotten. They walked down red carpets together in matching outfits and he even wrote a scathing heartbreak song about her after their relationship came to an end… Who remembers “Cry Me a River?” The relationship did not end on good terms.

1 Rihanna (Rumor) - 90.6 Million Followers

Rumors about a relationship between Justin Timberlake and Rihanna made their rounds in 2007 after they start together in the music video for her song “Rehab.” Rihanna blatantly denied the romance even though there are plenty of witnesses who saw them getting very flirtatious when the cameras were not rolling. Rihanna takes the cake when it comes to Instagram followers with 90.6 million at the moment.
