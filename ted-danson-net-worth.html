Ted Danson is an American actor, producer and author with an estimated net worth of $60 million.

Ted Danson is best recognized for his lead role as Sam Malone in Cheers and Dr. John Becker in Becker.  He currently appears in the drama series  CSI: Crime Scene Investigation which is aired in CBS. Once a regular cast in the HBO comedy series titled Bored To Death, Ted Danson is presently one of the casts of the legal drama titled Damages alongside Glenn Close and a recurring role in the sitcom Curb Your Enthusiasm.

In his 15 years of being in the industry, Ted Danson has earned a total of 15 Primetime Emmy Awards nominations which won him two, won three out of ten nominations for the Golden Globe Awards,  an American Comedy Award, and a nomination for the Screen Actors Guild Awards. He also won a star on Holywood's Walk of Fame.
