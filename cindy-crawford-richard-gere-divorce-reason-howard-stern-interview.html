Cindy Crawford Finally Revealed Why Her Relationship With Richard Gere Ended During An Interview Wit

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Highlights

  • Cindy Crawford and Richard Gere divorced after a short marriage in the 1990s.
  • Their divorce was driven by their different work schedules and lack of time spent together.
  • Despite rumors, Richard Gere's sexuality was not a concern during their marriage.

Supermodel Cindy Crawford and actor Richard Gere have always been extremely silent about the reason for their 1995 divorce. While much of the media world was focused on them when their relationship went public in the early 1990s (mostly due to their age difference), little is known about the end of their marriage. Especially since little information is out there about Richard Gere, in general. Partially due to drastic changes in his career.

But Crawford may have revealed a lot more about their breakup than fans know. In an unearthed interview with Howard Stern from the late 1990s, the acclaimed supermodel delved into her divorce from Richard Gere as well as some of the most intimate details about their relationship...

Cindy Crawford Revealed The Real Reason She Divorced Richard Gere During An Interview With Howard Stern

Cindy Crawford admitted that she was nervous to be interviewed by Howard Stern during her first of two appearances on his radio show. The first was in the 1990s when the absurdly rich radio legend was still on terrestrial radio and known for being a shock jock. While the 2015 interview on The Howard Stern Show on SiriusXM satellite radio was far more in-depth and adult, her '90s appearance was no holds barred. This included delving into her intimate life with her now ex-husband Richard Gere.

While the supermodel waited in the green room, Howard Stern, his long-time co-host Robin Quivers, producer Gary Dell'Abate, and head writer Fred Norris prepared the audience for what they were about to hear. This included talking about her 1995 divorce from Richard Gere, whom she had married just four years prior in 1991.

  • Cindy Crawford was 22 when she met Richard Gere, who was 17 years older than her
  • According to People, they had very little in common
  • Crawford credits Gere for teaching her how to be famous

"Let's face it, [Cindy] divorced Richard [Gere] because he has no sense of humor, he's a bore," Howard Stern said to his colleagues.

Howard Stern continued to bash Richard Gere when Cindy Crawford finally arrived in the studio.

"I knew it wouldn't last because I don't like Richard."

"I don't. I really don't," Stern said to Crawford. "I think he's a good actor and everything to watch on screen, but I think he's very pompous.

"You know what, he's not your type of guy," Cindy Crawford replied diplomatically.

"No," Stern stated. "And he's not yours either obviously. Now you agree with me, right?"

"No, I still like Richard."

"She says she doesn't want to see him because she might fall in love with him all over again," Robin Quivers chimed in.

"Yeah, well, don't believe everything you read, Robin," Crawford smiled.

"I still have feelings for Richard. But I was so young. It was kind of like my first great love."

Crawford revealed to Stern that not wanting to same thing is ultimately what drove her and Gere apart.

That and the fact that they were separated too much due to their very different work schedules. At the time, Richard Gere was really becoming an A-list movie star and Crawford was expanding on her supermodel status.

Her comments on The Howard Stern Show very much were in line with previous comments she made to People:

"We didn’t spend enough time together. And we’re equally responsible for that. We thought it would be okay if we just flew in from Paris to L.A. to get together for a night to see each other. It wasn’t."

Stern then pushed Crawford to admit that Gere dumped her. But this was something that she wouldn't confirm nor deny.

"Because, if you still love him, that means he dumped you," Stern said.

"Okay, well you can believe whatever you want," Crawford responded.

"What I'd hope to project is that we both decide that we both wanted -- it wasn't -- we weren't on the same path."

"I took mine and he took his. It was so painless. We didn't have one of those divorces."

Is Richard Gere Gay?

During Cindy Crawford's discussion with Howard Stern in the 1990s, the radio legend brought up the famous rumor that Richard Gere was gay. This is something Stern believed Gere was concerned about during his marriage with the supermodel.

"[Richard] wasn't concerned about the fact that people said he was gay," Crawford said of her ex-husband during their four-year marriage. "But I think that he didn't like the fact that people possibly thought that he lived a lie. Because he's always been doing this work on behalf of the Tibetans and he didn't want to lose his credibility."

"I think it's good what he does with the Tibetians," Stern added after saying he disliked Gere. "Yeah, I do."

The Richard Gere is gay rumors have been long since debunked. On top of this, Gere has a lengthy relationship history with numerous famous and non-famous women alike.

Cindy Crawford Revealed Intimate Details About Her Marriage To Richard Gere During Her Howard Stern Interview

Aside from delving into the reasons for their divorce, Cindy Crawford also revealed some juicy tidbits about her sex life with acclaimed actor, Richard Gere. At the time of her '90s interview, Howard Stern was very much known for his raunchy interview style. So, delving into this subject matter wasn't exactly surprising. Crawford knew it was coming and clearly wasn't afraid to spill the beans.

"So, after Richard Gere and you broke up -- I can see why you'd marry him -- because [you both] were so damn popular and everything," Stern said, trying to lead Crawford into the next line of questioning.

"And he's really good-looking," Robin Quivers said.

"But he has a small penis," Stern claimed.

"You don't know how much a guy will grow," Quivers said, referring to Gere's famous on-camera nude scenes.

"I just want to know how you know that?' Cindy Crawford asked.

"I saw the movie -- Breathless -- he's got nothing! He's got less than me," Howard Stern joked.

"They should have called it penis-less".

"Well, I watched the movie I think -- it wasn't that memorable," Crawford said. "To be honest with you, I hadn't had that much experience [with men before the relationship], so I guess it's all relative."

"You didn't say, 'oh my god?'"

"I think by the time I'd seen the movie, I'd seen it in real life," Crawford responded.

"So, you were dissatisfied on your wedding night?"

"Oh yeah, I waited until the wedding night even though we'd gone out [before] for years."

Despite Howard Stern poking and prodding about her sex life with Richard Gere, Cindy Crawford remained somewhat tight-lipped. However, she was far more honest about why their relationship ended.
