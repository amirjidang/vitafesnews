PIC: Dakota Fanning Goes Brunette!

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Looks like the leaves aren't the only things changing color this fall. Twilight actress Dakota Fanning, 18, has undergone a seasonal makeover, too, trading her sunny blonde locks for chestnut brown tresses.

Fanning, who broke out as a child star in the 2001 film I Am Sam and is now a student at New York University, showed off her new, darker look in a photo posted on Instagram over the weekend. The caption read simply, "Well look who turned into a brunette!"

This isn't Fanning's first experiment with a new hair color. She donned a dark wig to play Robert De Niro's daughter in the 2005 movie Hide and Seek, and earlier this year, she dyed the tips of her hair bright pink.
