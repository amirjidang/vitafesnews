A Peek Inside George Clooney's Lake Como Villa

It is no secret that American actor and filmmaker George Clooney is a massive fan and enthusiast of the Italian culture. The actor just fell in love with the Lake Como and made sure that he gets to buy the property when it was being sold by the Heinz family. Lake Como is famous and among the sought after locations when celebrities are trying to buy some properties in Italy. Clooney loves the location so much that he personally chose the Lake Como as the film location for the blockbuster film titled Ocean’s Twelve in 2004.

Long time bachelor George Clooney who had sworn off marriage publicly has surprised everyone when he proposed in 2014 to his girlfriend international human rights lawyer Amal Alamuddin. The lovely pair then married that year, the actor married the human rights lawyer Amal Alamuddin in Venice and Amal gave birth to twins named Ella and Alexander in 2017. Since he has become a family man, American actor and filmmaker have invested more in a family friendly properties rather than bachelor centric estate. Take a look at the beautiful family home of the Clooney family in Lake Como, Italy.

A Luxurious Villa In The Lap Of Italy

https://www.pinterest.ph/pin/35958497002213983/

Considering that the actor is among the most accomplished actors in Hollywood, it is not surprising that he owns a vast number of properties. Among them is the relaxed villa located on the Northern Italy’s Lombardy region, Lake Como. The couple reportedly stays their time off relaxing at the private abode every summer with their twins. The massive villa runs through the Lake Como and is surrounded by tons of stunning buildings and houses of the picturesque town of Laglio. The actor purchased the 18th-century home way back in 2001 from the Heinz family for $7 million.. At the time, it caught the attention of both the media and fans that often flock the entrance of the property in the hopes of seeing Clooney or even his family members. The property has 25 rooms in total.

The Ocean’s Eleven actor reportedly purchased the adjacent villa called Villa Margherita to help secure his privacy and the privacy of everyone staying at the villa. The town of Laglio also welcomes Clooney and his family very dearly that in 2015, the mayor of Laglio decided to issue some restricting order to stop the fans, the paparazzi and the media for ever approaching the luxury property. The lawbreaker will be expected to pay a fine of $544 even if they are 100 meters away from the property. The individuals not related to Clooney or not invited to the villa should maintain a distance to make sure they will not face some trouble.

A Beautiful Getaway Estate

https://www.pinterest.ph/pin/717339046881024514/

The three story luxury property boasts an incredible view of the Lake Como. One of its features that the sunset can be seen clearly inside the home. The residence of the Academy Award winner actor has a handful of amazing features including a beautiful balcony and some secluded outdoor patios. The actor and his wife also decided to construct a large wall on the outside of the property to keep the water out and avoid some flooding.

It also contains features which can be usually seen in some luxury mansions like tennis courts, pool, gym and garage where the actor reportedly stores some of his extensive collection of his vintage motorbikes. It was reported that he has since invested millions into making the massive property more suitable to their family. The luxurious villa is said to be the sleeping nest of Clooney at noon.

The Frequent Gatherings At Clooney’s Lake Como Property

https://www.pinterest.ph/pin/281475045436219528/

The family of George Clooney has been spending some time every summer at their villa in Como even before he got married to Amal. It has been their family tradition to just relaxed and enjoy each other’s company every year. It was also reported that they usually hosts some of their A-lister friends and prominent figures in the property including former president Barack Obama, Prince Harry, Meghan Markle, Jennifer Aniston, and Matt Damon. Even the family of Amal has been spotted at the property and her father Ramzi Alamuddin even held his birthday at the luxury villa. It was reported though that he is selling the Lake Como house before the birth of his beloved twins however the reports were debunked and was claimed untrue since Clooney still owns the property even to this day.

In May 2021, the couple has even invited everyone to come hang out with them. The lovely couple decided to join the team of the charitable giving meets fundraising website called Omaze. They offered their abode to the people who want to take a vacation on Italy's stunning Lake Como. One has to enter and donate to charity to enter into the lottery where the lucky winner will be able to stay at the couple’s lovely villa.
