Sting Pulled From AEW Double Or Nothing Fan Fest Due To Injury

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 4, 2024

is currently dealing with an injury.

In a tweet on May 25, All Elite Wrestling announced that the legend has not been cleared to travel due to an injury. As a result, he will be unable to attend the Meet & Greets at the AEW Double or Nothing Fan Fest on May 28.

Due to injury last week, @Sting has not been cleared to travel & will unfortunately not be at this Saturday’s #AEWDoN Fan Fest Meet & Greets. For fans who purchased tickets to see #TheIcon, Watch your inbox for information on refunds

— All Elite Wrestling (@AEW) May 26, 2022

On the May 18 episode of AEW Dynamite, Sting was one of the Undisputed Elite’s target in a post-match beatdown. Kyle O’Reilly smashed his ankle to conclude the show. It’s currently unclear whether Sting’s injury is legitimate or a part of a storyline.

Sting, 63, has won all nine of his matches since he returned to the ring at AEW Revolution 2021. Most recently, he teamed up with Darby Allin and The Hardys to defeat Private Party, The Butcher, and The Blade on the March 23 episode of AEW Dynamite.

WrestleZone will provide more details as they become available.
