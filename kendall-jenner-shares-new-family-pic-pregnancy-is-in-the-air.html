Kendall Jenner Shares New Family Pic: Pregnancy Is in the Air

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, August 16, 2024

Not interested! Kendall Jenner posted a pic with her family and made it clear that she won’t be adding a baby to the mix.

In the Friday, April 26, Instagram upload, the model, 23, smiled on a couch between other Keeping Up With the Kardashians cast members and their little ones. While the photo was captioned with a heart, the next slide featured a meme of a person wearing a plastic bag. The caption? “Pregnancy is in the air. Me.”

Kylie Jenner sat next to the reality star, with Travis Scott at her side, their 14-month-old daughter, Stormi, on their laps. Kim Kardashian was on Kendall’s other side, holding her 3-year-old, Saint, and her 15-month old daughter, Chicago. Her husband, Kanye West, stood behind them with their 5-year-old daughter, North, on his shoulders.

Kourtney Kardashian and Khloé Kardashian rounded off the end of the couch. 12-month-old True sat on her mom’s lap, while the Poosh creator, 40, held 4-year-old Reign, who was reaching for 9-year-old Mason’s hand. Kourtney’s 6-year-old daughter, Penelope, was missing from the photo. Rob Kardashian and his daughter, Dream, 2, were also MIA.

Matriarch Kris Jenner, though, smiled for the sweet shot. Her boyfriend, Corey Gamble, stood beside her.

The last time most of the Kardashian-Jenner clan was pictured together was for their 2018 Christmas card shoot. Kendall was on the outs for that photoshoot.
