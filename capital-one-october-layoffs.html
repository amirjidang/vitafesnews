Carta’s History

Carta, established in 2012, has emerged as a prominent player in the equity management technology industry. With a suite of innovative solutions, Carta’s platform offers real-time valuations, cap table management, and various services to facilitate efficient equity and stock option management within companies. The company’s exponential growth and success are evident in its current valuation of $7.4 billion.

In helping businesses streamline their equity management processes, Carta’s technology has revolutionized the industry by providing accurate, up-to-date data and insights. Through their platform, companies can effortlessly manage their equity transactions, track ownership changes, and ensure compliance with regulatory frameworks.

Carta’s commitment to leveraging cutting-edge technology and delivering exceptional solutions has positioned them as a leader in equity management, empowering companies to make informed decisions regarding their equity structure.
