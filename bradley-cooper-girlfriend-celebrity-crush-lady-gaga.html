These Celebrities Want To Be Bradley Cooper's Next Girlfriend

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 25, 2024

There's an endless thirst for Bradley Cooper. Of course, fans think the Hangover and A Star Is Born actor is just about the hottest star in Hollywood. But so do several celebrities. Bradley has certainly dated his fair share of famous women, namely his baby-mama model Irina Shayk who he's currently co-parenting his daughter with. But Bradley's relationship history also includes actor Jennifer Esposito, model Suki Waterhouse, Star Trek star Zoe Saldana, Renee Zellwegger, and he's even had a brief fling with Cameron Diaz. So, yeah, Bradley has gotten around a little bit. But the man could easily find a new girlfriend in Hollywood if he chooses...

Those famous women aside, there are countless other beautiful, talented, and noteworthy celebrities who've openly flirted with Bradley. Most of whom appear to be absolutely down to date him should he ever be looking. These are the famous people who seem to be lusting after the acclaimed actor, singer, and director...

10 Lady Gaga

The most obvious celebrity on this list is Lady Gaga. So much has been made out of whatever did or didn't happen between the two co-stars. In an interview with Oprah, Lady Gaga said, "Quite frankly, I think the press is very silly. I mean, we made a love story. For me, as a performer and as an actress, of course, we wanted people to believe that we were in love." But, seriously, there's also no denying that she waxes poetic about him and love experts seem to think she was into him.

9 Selena Gomez

During an interview for her 2013 album, "Stars Dance", Selena Gomez has asked about meeting Bradley Cooper. The interviewer claimed that he couldn't get any words out when he met him. Selena said she experienced the same thing when she came face-to-face with the handsome Hangover star. "I just remember seeing him and kind of freaking out and running away," Selena admitted," He was so beautiful."

8 Robin Quivers

Howard Stern's longtime co-host Robin Quivers has freely admitted to many of her celebrity crushes. Not only that but she's flirted with them whenever they appear on the acclaimed SiriusXM radio show for an interview with The King Of All Media. Bradley Cooper is one of her favorites. On numerous occasions, she's told Howard about how handsome she finds Bradley. In an 'F, Marry, Kill' game, she claimed she wanted to 'F' Bradley.

7 Amy Schumer

The now-married and new mother, Amy Schumer, admitted to her crush on Bradley when she was on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon. She claimed that Bradley is the type of guy who stares directly into your eyes and asks you questions when you're talking to him. Amy also said that she feels uncomfortable talking to somebody that handsome yet loves to lustfully yell out his name whenever she sees him at events.

6 Taylor Swift

According to Radar Online in 2013, Taylor Swift asked Jennifer Lawrence, a friend and frequent co-star of Bradley's, if she could introduce her to the star. While this report has not been legitimized by Taylor herself, enough information about Taylor lusting after Bradley exists. Still, this moment was a flash in the pan as Taylor swiftly moved on to crushing on (and dating) other major celebrities. For the record, the same source claims that Bradley had no interest in meeting Taylor and had to politely decline.

5 Wendy Williams

Love her or hate her, daytime talk show host Wendy Williams is always open about her feelings and opinions. And her opinion of Bradley Cooper is that he's straight-up hot. In fact, she claims that Bradley is one of her biggest celebrity crushes.

4 Natalie Maines

During an interview on Ellen DeGeneres's talk show, acclaimed singer Natalie Maines of The Chicks (formerly The Dixie Chicks) got caught up in a game of "Who'd You Rather Date?" Natalie chose Bradley over both Brad Pitt, The Weeknd, Jake Gyllenhaal, and Leonardo DiCaprio. When Bradley was pitted up against Lenny Kravitz, Natalie admitted that she had grown up being in love with Lenny. In fact, she was convinced that she was going to marry him one day... Natalie still went with Bradley.

3 Hannah Brown

Ellen DeGeneres' "Who'd You Rather Date" game also exposed Hannah B's crush on Bradley Cooper. The former Bachelorette star picked Bradley over Luke Bryan, Zac Efron, Ryan Gosling, Chris Pine, Idris Elba, and Channing Tatum. Hannah admitted that she loves Bradley's "sweaty" look in A Star Is Born and that's the version of him that she'd like to date.

2 Bethenny Frankel

Once again, Ellen exposed another celebrity's crush on Bradley Cooper. This time it was former Real Housewives star, Bethenny Frankel. Bradley made it to the end of her list, but he also got kicked off by Jake Gyllenhall. Bethenny claimed the choice between the two was almost impossible. Still, Bradley won out.

1 Bang Chan

K-Pop star Bang Chan has also admitted to having a thing for the Star Is Born actor. In an interview, the South Korean superstar said, "One thing that I have to point out is, Bradley Cooper’s eyes are so beautiful." After Bang made these comments, they immediately started trending on Twitter. Clearly, his fans were all for shipping him with Bradley Cooper. Unfortunately for Bang, that doesn't seem to be in the cards.
