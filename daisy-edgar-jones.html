Daisy Edgar-Jones Height, Weight, Age, Boyfriend, Biography &amp; Family

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Daisy Edgar-Jones (born May 24, 1998) is an English Actress, Theatre Artist, and Social Media Personality from Islington, London. She first garnered immense popularity after starring in the TV series “War of the Worlds” produced by Fox Networks Group.

The actress performed one of the main characters as Emily Gresham. It was directed by Richard Clark and Gilles Coulier. The program was written by Howard Overman. Furthermore, it was loosely based on the H.G. Wells 1898 novel of the same name.

The first episode of the hit show was premiered on October 28, 2019. Some of the other main casts of War of the Worlds are Elizabeth McGovern as Helen Brown, Gabriel Byrne as Bill Ward, Léa Drucker as Catherine Duran, Natasha Little as Sarah Gresham, etc.

Filmography, TV Shows & Movies

In 2016, the talented actress made her acting debut with a British comedy-drama television series “Cold Feet”. Mike Bullen worked as a creator of the program. Daisy got an opportunity to play one of the recurring roles as Olivia Marsden from 2016 to 2020 alongside James Nesbitt, Robert Bathurst, John Thomson, etc.

Later, the artist also appeared in the Christmas Special “Outnumbered” as Kate. Edgar-Jones then starred only in one show called “Silent Witness” created by Nigel McCreery in 2017.

The actress acted for a minor part as Jessica Timpson in two episodes of the show named Discovery Part 1 and Part 2. The following year, she made her pictorial debut with a 2018 drama film “Pond Life” written by Richard Cameron.

Bill Buckhurst worked as a director of the movie which was released on 14 November 2018. Daisy portrayed one of the major parts as Cassie alongside Tom Varey as Trev, Esme Creed-Miles as Pogo, Angus Imrie as Malcolm, Shaun Dooley as Rus, Faye McKeever as Tracey, etc.

Career & Hulu Series – Normal People

Her career’s breakthrough role came with 2020 TV series called “Normal People”. It is a series developed for Hulu and BBC Three by Element Pictures. Normal People based on the novel of the same name by Sally Rooney.

In addition, the show came out on April 26, 2020, on BBC Three in the United Kingdom. Later, on April 27, 2020, it was released on BBC One. In Ireland, the program has been airing on RTÉ One from April 28, 2020.

The following day, the series was officially released on the Hulu platform. The actress is portraying one of the main roles of Marianne Sheridan in Normal People. She is working opposite Paul Mescal (Irish Actor) who is performing as Connell Waldron.

Some of the other recurring casts of Normal People are Sarah Greene as Lorraine, Aislín McGuckin as Denise, Leah McNamara as Rachel, Fionn O’Shea as Jamie, Eanna Hardwicke as Rob, etc.
