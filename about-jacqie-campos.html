All About Jacqie Campos, Jenni Riveras Daughter

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Jacqie Campos is the daughter of the late Latina and popular American Singer, Jenni Rivera. The singer met her demise in a plane crash which shocked the world in late 2012. Now, her daughter makes the news with her new accomplishments.

Who Is Jacqie Rivera?

Jacqie Campos before her adopted current surname was known as Jacqie Melina Marin Rivera. She is one of the daughters of the late and famous Jenni Rivera. Jenni was one of the famous Latin American singers who gained fame by her voice and met her unfortunate demise in a plane crash.

Now, Jacqie Campos is carrying on her mother’s legacy by turning out to be a talented and skilled singer. She is considered to be an upcoming artist who is quickly making her way and proving to be a recognizable force in the whole music industry.

Jacqui Rivera’s Career

The Rivera name has been linked to fame and entertainment since quite some time. Jacqie was featured on the reality family-based show for TV titled The Riveras. The popular TV show featured and had Jenni Rivera’s children.

The show highlighted how each of them are pursuing their own life choices and honoring the legacy of the Rivera surname. 

The show went on to run till 24th September 2020. 

Jacqie has also appeared on another TV reality show titled I Love Jenni. The TV series was aired from 2011 to around 2 more years till 2013. 

In the year 2012, she also appeared in Chiquis ‘N’ Control.

Venturing Into the Music Industry

Jacqie has also ventured into the music industry. Taking steps after her mother, she has labeled herself in the industry and a known and recognized one. 

Jenni Rivera’s Troubled First Marriage

Jenni Rivera’s marriage was troubled with her then-husband Jose. They both got married in 1984. Their marriage resulted in three children: Chiquis, Jacqie and Michael. Chiquis Rivera was born on 26th June 1985 and Michael Marin was born on 11th September 1991. The marriage then turned to divorce after eight years due to constant and increasing emotional abuse.

The reasons for divorce were multiple fold. Jose molested the children and his own sister-in-law Rosie. He also used to mistreat his wife, Jenni Rivera, and there were no signs of stopping. Jose’s punishment for his actions were sentenced to a jail time of 30.

Jenni Rivera’s Second Marriage

After getting a divroce from Jose, Jacqie went on to marry again. She tied the knot with Juan Lopez. This reunion culminated in Jacqie’s other siblings: Jenicka and Juan Angel. 

Personal Life of Jacqie Campos

Jacqie Rivera had a very troubled and traumatic childhood. 

Born on 20th November 1989, Jacqie was born to Jose Trinidad Marin, her father. Contrast to the beautiful location of Long Beach, California, United States of America, her childhood was scarred and traumatized by continuous sexual molestations from her father.

Not only with her, but Jose used to sexually molest her elder sister as well. 

Coming to Terms with her Past and her Father

It wasn’t until 2017 when Jacqie Campos met her father, José Trinidad Marín again. She decided she wanted to forget and move on from her emotional traumas she experienced as a child. 

After moving on, she met her father in the year 2017 and chose to forgive both her parents: Jenni Rivera and Jose. She even posted photos of herself and her father’s meeting on social media with her father. 

Jacqie Rivera’s Relationship With Danny Yanez

Before she became Jacqie Campos, she was involved with Danny Yanez. The couple were involved together and were serious enough that they decided to have a child together. 

Jacqie had a daughter named Jaylah Yanez from her companionship with Danny Yanez. However, they soon broke up and Jacqie would move on to establish a serious relationship with her next lover and soon-to-be husband, Mike Campos.

From Jacqie Rivera to Jacqie Campos

Jacqie Rivera updated her surname and changed it to Campos when she decided to marry her lover Mike Campos. 

Jacqie Rivera started to date and like Mike Campos. The couple decided to marry in the year 2012. The marriage went smoothly for around 5 years and they both had beautiful babies together. 

Jacqie Campos had three children altogether: Jenavieve Campos born on 21st March 2014, Jordan Campos born on 6th April 2016  and Julian Campos born on 27th April 2020. Their children are lovely and followers simply adore the trio. 

The Divorce And Rumors After

However, just after five years of becoming parents and their marriage. Jacqie and Mike Campos decided they wanted to split. 

The couple divorced in 2017. Followers were shocked by the news.

After some time, Jacqie shared a picture of the whole family together. This has sparked rumors and speculation that maybe Jacqie and Mike Campos have reconciled and are thinking of getting back together. Who wouldn’t want a family to be reunited again?

Bond of the Siblings

Jacqie and all of her siblings share a wonderful and friendly bond. Jacqie is also seen frequently sharing pictures of herself along with her siblings as if to highlight the unique relationship they have with each other. 

Even after the first three children lost their mother in 2012, they went through the heavy driven ordeal together and proved they were unshakeable. 

Marriage with Juan Manuel Lopez Pardo and the two children which it produced (Jenicka Lopez in 1997 and Johnny Lopez in 2001) share their bond as well.

All five children have a great and exemplary bond with each other. Jacqie’s frequent images posted on her social media are evident of their relationship and demonstrates that whatever happened in between their parents or in their lives, family matters were there to stay.

Jacqie Rivera’a Active Social Life

The career of Jacqie was sure to garner some buzz. She has a sizable following on her social media accounts where she is also active in sharing her various activities. 

Her Instagram page has almost 2 million followers and her Facebook page has more than 600 thousand followers. She also has a YouTube channel which has more than around 400 thousand subscribers. 

Her active social life is where her fans also get a peek of her personal life.
