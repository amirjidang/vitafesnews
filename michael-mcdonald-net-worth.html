Michael McDonald is a well-known singer, songwriter, and musician with a net worth of around $40 million. He was born on February 12th, 1959 and started his singing career as a backup vocalist in 1970. Soon after that, he joined “The Dobbie Brothers” in 1976 and stayed with the group until 1982. This time was one of the best periods for the band and during this period the band was able to produce so many hit songs.

After leaving The Doobie Brothers, Michael McDonald released his first solo album in 1982 which received good response from audience and critics both. After that, he performed with several other artists and he released a lot of other music albums and solo hits as well. In addition to this, he also founded an independent recording company. During his music career, he won 5 Grammy awards and many other awards.
