Central Cee Brother and Family, How Many Siblings Does He Have?

Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Central Cee has a brother named Juke Caesar, and the two siblings have shared a track, “Lil Bro,” in the rapper’s mixtape 23.

Central Cee, whose real name is Oakley Neil H T Caesar-Su, is an English rapper and songwriter. Oakley was born and raised in Shepherd’s Bush, London, where he started his career as a rapper and songwriter.

Cee started writing songs at the age of just eight years old and recorded his first son at the age of 14. He went to the studio with one of his school friends to record his first song, and his career changed forever.

The rapper was exposed to Hip Hop, Rap, R&B, and Reggae music from childhood as his father often played those genres at home while picking him up from school.

Central Cee Brother: How Many Siblings Does He Have?

Central Cee’s brother’s name is Juke Caesar, according to Rolling Stone.

The rapper Central Cee is the eldest of four siblings, but much information about his other two younger brothers has not come into the limelight.

Cee has shared a song on his mixtape 23 with his little brother Juke, the song titled “Lil bro” is the closing song on his mixtape and the two siblings verse back to each other.

The rapper is trying to keep his baby brother off the streets by explaining his situation and how he was misled.

This is not the first or only time Central’s brother has been mentioned in a song. Digga D, another well-known English rapper, released a diss track about how he attacked Central’s brother, and the rapper was standing there helpless.

While Digga’s diss track does not mention which brother he is talking about, fans quickly deduce the rapper is talking about Juke in his song.

Central has not revealed anything about his other siblings on the web yet. He tries to keep his family and siblings away from the web or his spotlight to avoid unwanted media attention.

The rapper was also rumored to be a relative of Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu, a Turkish actress and Love Island contestant, but the actress was quick to shut down all the rumors.

Rapper Central Cee Age and Wiki

Rapper Central Cee’s actual age is 24 years old as of 2022. He was born on 4th June 1998.

According to his date of birth, Cee has acquired Gemini as his zodiac sign. People with Gemini as their zodiac sign tend to be flexible, extroverted, and clever.

The rapper grew up in Shepherd’s Bush in London. He started his music career early in his life. By the time he was 14, he had already recorded his first song after visiting a recording studio with his school friend.

He attended Chelsea Academy in childhood and was a few years senior to fellow rapper Digga D. According to Cee’s Wikipedia profile, the rapper kept to himself at school but sometimes lost his temper with his classmates.

The rapper left home early. He was already living around with his friends when he recorded his first song. He realized he could achieve more when he wandered off into different parts of London on his own and realized how big the world was from his imagination.

Apart from making music, the rapper worked in a shoe store before quitting after just three weeks when he knew about his wages.

Central Cee Parents and Family

Talking about his parents, Central Cee was born to an Irish mother and a Guyanese father.

His parents played a vital role in shaping his today’s successful music career during his childhood.

The rapper’s father would often play music around him, creating a musical atmosphere around the already talented rapper at home.

However, Cee has never revealed the names of his parents to his followers or on the web. As we mentioned, the rapper prefers to keep his family members’ and his parents’ identities away from the internet to avoid unwanted media attention.

His grandparents are of Biblian Ecuador descent, and his great-grandfather was Arawak, indigenous peoples of northern South America and the Caribbean.

The rapper has three other siblings in his family. All of whom are younger than him. He also has cousins in his family, but the rapper has maintained a distance from them since childhood.
