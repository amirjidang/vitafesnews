REVOLVER DRIP Results (3/4): KUSHIDA vs. Jonathan Gresham

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

On March 4, held their REVOLVER DRIP event at the Horizon Event Center in Clive, Iowa. The event featured stars like KUSHIDA, Jonathan Gresham, and Alex Shelley, and it aired on FITE TV.

The results, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  • Steve Maclin (c) def. Rich Swann
  • Rocky Romero (c) def. Lince Dorado and Jake Crist
  • Manders def. Crash Jaxon
  • Alex Shelley (c) def. Matthew Palmer
  • The Unit (Logan James, Tyler Matrix & JT Dunn) def. Death Dollz (Taya Valkyrie, Jessicka, & Rosemary)
  • Madman Fulton (with Matthew Rehwoldt) def. Dan The Dad
  • Johnny Revolver def. Ninja Mack
  • KUSHIDA def. Jonathan Gresham
  • The Rascalz (Zachary Wentz & Trey Miguel) def. BULLET CLUB (Chris Bey & Ace Austin) (with Gia Miller) (c) and The Second Gear Crew (Matthew Justice & Mance Warner) to win the titles
