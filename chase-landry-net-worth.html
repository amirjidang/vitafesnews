He captained his own ship across the Atchafalaya River Basin, with his cousin Holden as his deckhand. The Landry family focuses on gator hunting during the hunting season and shrimp fishing during the off-season.

Aside from his career in hunting and fishing, Chase Landry also owns a gas station and a restaurant called Chasin’ Tails.
