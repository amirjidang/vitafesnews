'Fantastic Four': Which Original Cast Member Has The Highest Net Worth Today?

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, August 30, 2024

In 2005, Fox released comic book movie Fantastic Four before the booming business that is Marvel became a cultural phenomenon. Despite not being connected to the MCU, this Marvel film is one of two versions of the franchise (not including the 1994 unaired film) and the more widely accepted of the two.

Related: Steps Disney Must Take To Ensure The Success Of The Fantastic Four In The MCU

This film was also a box office success, earning $333 million worldwide. A follow-up was produced in 2007, titled Fantastic Four: Rise of the Silver Surfer. Due to the sequel’s flop (along with some contract issues with certain cast members) the third film was cancelled and the series came to a screeching halt only to be revived in 2015 via reboot. But even though Fantastic Four came to an end over a decade ago, the cast has since stayed in the spotlight and have the bills to prove it.

7 Ioan Gruffudd - $5 Million

Ioan Gruffudd, known to most Marvel fans as the original Mr. Fantastic, plays scientist Reed Richards in the film. Since his days as the ever twisty rubber man, Gruffudd has played numerous roles including Lancelot in King Arthur, William in Amazing Grace, as well as Kevin in 102 Dalmations. He also took to the small screen for a ride as he has appeared in the CW’s Ringer, Lifetime’s Unreal, and Liar. Ioan has also played a medical examiner twice in ABC’s Forever and Harrow, both times with a dangerous secret that threatens to unravel. Over the years, Ioan has managed to secure a net worth of $5 million.

6 Michael Chiklis - $14 Million

When comic book fans think of The Thing, their minds definitely go to Michael Chiklis. And whether that’s a compliment or an insult, fans agree that Chiklis’ Ben Grimm was one of the most accurate casting choices in Marvel history. Since his stint as this lovable rock beast, he has gone on to appear in films like The Do-Over, Rupture, Hubie Halloween and biopic The Three Stooges. He is best known for his roles in the Emmy-winning series The Shield, ABC’s No Ordinary Family, The Commish, Gotham, and the fourth season of American Horror Story. With all these acting credits, it’s no wonder that Chiklis has gained a net worth of $14 million.

5 Julian McMahon - $16 Million

In this film, Julian McMahon captured the controlling nature of supervillain Dr. Doom with an additional desperate need for help with his transformation. Besides playing Victor Von Doom, he is known for his appearances in shows like Home & Away, Charmed, and Nip/Tuck. He even dabbled in the world of television crime with his roles in Profiler and FBI: Most Wanted. He has since returned to his Marvel roots with his role as possessed Jonah in Hulu's Runaways. Julian MacMahon has reached a net worth of $16 million.

4 Kerry Washington - $50 Million

Playing one of the minor characters in the film, Kerry Washington embraced the notion that beauty is in the eye of the beholder when playing Ben Grimm’s blind love interest. She is best known for her role as the smooth and fierce Olivia Pope in ABC’s Scandal, which earned her not only an Emmy nomination but a Golden Globe as well. She has also appeared in Django Unchained, The Dead Girl, Save the Last Dance, For Colored Girls, and American Son. In 2016, her net worth was estimated to be around $13.5 million but it has since tripled. In 2018, Forbes named her the eighth highest paid actresses in television so it’s no wonder she’s secured a $50 million dollar net worth.

3 Stan Lee - $50 Million

A comic book writer, editor and producer, Stan Lee was one of the most well known writers in history with his work for the Marvel comics. And while acting wasn't his desired calling, he appeared in a cameo in most Marvel films (until his passing in 2018 and subsequent last appearance in 2019’s Avengers: Endgame). In this film, Stan Lee appears as a character he created, mailman Willie Lumpkin, which he revealed was one of his favorite cameos up until the creation of Avengers: Age of Ultron. He would later appear as another postman in Captain America: Civil War. He has also appeared in numerous non-Marvel projects and games. Before his death, he was stated to have a net worth of $50 million.

2 Chris Evans - $80 Million

Despite playing the funny and charming Johnny Storm, this would not be Chris Evans’ most prominent Marvel role. Instead, this Human Torch would go on to be known as Steve Rogers / Captain America in three solo films and 8 other MCU projects. And despite being hesitant to take on the role because of his experience in this superhero film, Evans soon skyrocketed to stardom. He has appeared in comic book films like Scott Pilgrim Vs. The World, The Losers and Snowpiercer. He is also known for his roles in Before We Go, Gifted, The Red Sea Diving Resort, and Knives Out. He is set to appear as iconic spaceman Buzz in the animated movie Lightyear and in the upcoming comedy Don’t Look Up. With all of these acting credits and along with big bucks he received from Marvel (as Avengers: Endgame is the second highest grossing movie of all time), Chris Evans has secured a net worth of $80 million.

1 Jessica Alba - Estimated $200 Million

A household name, Jessica Alba rose to fame with her role as the Invisible Woman Sue Storm and quickly became a staple in box office hits. She has appeared in films like Good Luck Chuck, Little Fockers, Mechanic: Resurrection, and Spy Kids: All The Time in The World. She is also known for her role in crime series LA’s Finest until 2020. Most of her money does not come from her numerous acting roles, but her work in the business world. In 2011, Jessica Alba co-founded The Honest Company which sells infant and home goods. This business empire has had quite the history and while it hasn’t always been steady, it is now on the rise (and making millions). With all her efforts, Jessica Alba has an estimated net worth of as low as $200 million and as high as $314 million.
