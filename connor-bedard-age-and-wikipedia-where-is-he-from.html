Connor Bedard Age And Wikipedia: Where Is He From?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Despite his young age Connor Bedard has stunned all the people with his exceptional skills and talent. Learn more about the hockey player.

Bedard is a Canadian Hockey player who plays center for the Regina Pats of the Western Hockey League.

He is also the first hockey player who at the age of 15 was able to qualify to play in the WHL and will be eligible for the 2023 NHL Draft.

The young player was selected by the Regina Pats as first overall in the 2020 WHL Bantam Draft.

Connor Bedard Age and Height

Connor Bedard is 15 years of age, as of May 2021.

He was born in the year 2005.

He celebrated his birthday on July 17 and is from North Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.

The young teenage athlete has abundant potential as shown by many of the matches he has played and is still growing physically.

According to eliteprospects, Connor Bedard’s current height is 5 feet 9 inches and he weighs 181 lbs. 

The numbers are bound to grow as the hockey player has still got many years to grow.

Connor Bedard Parents and Sister

Connor Bedard’s parents are Melanie and Tom Bedard. 

As per this site, it is revealed that his family tends not to talk much about hockey while they are in their home.

With all he has achieved, it is no doubt that the family is brimming with pride and joy.
