After Buying A $23 Million Estate, Conan OBrien Beach House Has Been Finally Sold For $16.5 Milli

Posted by Patria Henriques on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

American late-night talk-show personality and comedian Conan O’Brien sells his beach home for $16.5 million after listing it in July. The oceanfront property, located south of Santa Barbara in Carpinteria, CA, was sold after the former SNL writer got the tempting offer. The host’s home stands just a few steps from the sand and he’s sure the buyer is going to enjoy it.

Conan and his wife, Liza, had sold the 2,142 square foot pad initially. However, they bought it back in 2015 for $7.9 million. This time around, the house has gone for grabs, and it’s likely not getting back to them again.

According to TMZ, Conan's been on the hunt for a buyer since July. He had listed the property in Carpinteria, CA, just south of Santa Barbara, for $16,500,000.

Conan's 2,142 square foot pad is just steps from the sand it underwent an extensive remodel after he and his wife Liza bought it back in 2015 -- at the time they plunked down $7.9M for the property.

The house has panoramic ocean, island and coastline views there's a large deck with a built-in BBQ, plus a sunset view deck with private stairs down to the beach.

The main house has two bedrooms, a gym and a detached, 2-bedroom guest house with a wraparound deck. The garage has a Tesla charging station too!

For those salt lifers there's an outdoor shower and storage area for surf and paddle boards essentials for life at the beach.

Conan O’Brien had to sell his beach home for a whopping $16.5 million after it was remodeled. The house has a panoramic and coastline view as there’s a large deck with an in-built BBQ. There’s also a view deck with private stairs leading to the beach.

The main house includes two bedrooms and a detached two-bedroom guest house with a wraparound deck. Also, there’s a gym and a Tesla charging station. For the swimmers and surfers, there’s an outdoor shower and storage space for surfboards and other items.

The reason Conan O’Brien had to sell his former Carpinteria beach home is unclear. But one distinctive feature is that the newly acquired $23 million dollar seaside home is bigger in size. It’s also a four-bedroom, however, it’s sitting on 3,400 square feet of living space.

The property was previously owned by a Connecticut-based restructuring specialist, Tim Coleman. It was built in the early 1960s; sitting on over an acre of land with a great view of the ocean.

The sold and newly acquired properties aren’t the only ones owned by the TBS star. The star also reportedly owns another $20 million Pacific Palisades mansion.
