A Look Inside The Millionaire Lifestyle Of Paul Logan

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Quick Links

  • Living The Millionaire Life
  • An Avid Collector
  • Splurging On Accessories And Parties

Logan was only ten years old when he began creating videos on Vine from the backyard of his childhood home in Ohio. After the discontinuation of Vine, he switched to YouTube and uploaded content on two channels. His slapstick humor, Midwestern charm, and strategically planned viral videos have earned him a fan following and sponsors that pay him millions to advertise and sponsor their products. Through his $19 million net worth, he has been able to make hefty purchases and live a lavish lifestyle as a 26-year old. Through his social media accounts, fans constantly get a glimpse of his life, friends, larger-than-life parties, and big purchases.

From buying a multi-million-dollar mansion in LA to investing in NFTs and buying vintage collections, Logan's lifestyle defines his persona.

Living The Millionaire Life

Starting with his real estate purchases, the YouTuber is known to have two real estate properties. The first big purchase occurred in 2017 when he bought a $6.6 million mansion in Encino, Los Angeles, as noted by Dirt. In the 1970s, The original home got constructed, and he made several renovations. The traditional ranch-style mansion has soaring ceilings, four fireplaces, a wine cellar, an expansive kitchen, a soundproof screening room, and a shaded backyard.

Logan moved out of his Los Angeles home to Puerto Rico to practice in peace to focus on becoming a professional boxer. He lives in a $13 million mansion on the island that is costing him $55,000 per month. The five-bedroom, seven-bathroom abode features modern decor and an ornate gazebo and swimming pool. According to the New York Post, the executive resort house is located in a gated community that allows access to tropical gardens, golf courses, and beaches.

Along with expensive homes, Paul also has a stunning car collection in his garage. He owns a white Mercedes Benz G550 4X4 worth $200,000, a customized Mercedes Maverick Sprinter Van featuring a PlayStation and a coffee machine. The latter cast cost him $150,000. The first car he purchased was a Dodge Challenger SRT8 that came with a $70,000 price tag which he later gifted to one of his fans. The coolest automobile purchase was an old-school bus he bought for $110,000. With the help of West Coast Customs, he changed the roof windows and renovated the tone of the bus.

An Avid Collector

The artist has always used his social media accounts to show fans his insane purchases. In December 2021, Logan went on Twitter to show his year-around NFT purchases in 2021. In August 2021, he spent $95,000 and $60,000 on two NFTs that looked like pixelated rocks. By the end of the year, he had bought 139 NFTs from 16 different collections. The purchases came to a total of $2.645 million. The most expensive NFT he purchased was for 188.00 ETH, which was the first collection he bought titled World Of Women.

Pokémon TCG cards have gained popularity around the world in recent years. As a Pokémon enthusiast, he purchased a few expensive cards from collector’s editions. In March 2021, Paul opened 36 rare first-edition packs of Pokémon cards on his live stream. The twelve holographic cards in the collection were worth a whopping $2 million, as stated by Screen Rant.

Splurging On Accessories And Parties

Another aspect of his lavish lifestyle includes wearing luxurious accessories that define him. Logan also has a stunning watch collection that includes a Richard Mille RM-11 Felipe Massa, the automated winding movement timepiece with a starting price of $289,000. As mentioned by Rolex Magazine, in 2017, to celebrate his last day in Dubai with a bang, Paul purchased a Rolex Day-Date II with an ice-blue dial and Roman numerals. He purchased the watch for $40,000, and the timepiece price has now surged to $80,000.

The YouTuber always trends on social media for the expensive parties he has thrown for himself and his friends as he loves to celebrate in style. In June 2019, Paul claimed to have thrown the World’s Best Party. The party was called Logan’s Luau and had a $75,000 budget and an additional cost to bring 50,000 pounds of sand to turn the backyard into a Hawaiian beach. With over 200 guests invited, the party featured a tiki bar, fire dancers, and even a mechanical shark.

From expensive homes to throwing lavish parties, Paul has a lifestyle envied by many. His bold lifestyle reflects what he likes, and fans love to get a glimpse into his life through his presence on social media. The content creator has come a long way from recording videos in his childhood home’s backyard and continues to create content that shocks and amazes the internet.
