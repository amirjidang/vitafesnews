Who Are Sean Andrew Kibaki’s Parents- Jimmy Kibaki and Sheryl Anne?

Sean Andrew Kibaki was born to his parents, Jimmy Kibaki and Sheryl Anne in October 1993 in Nairobi. He is the grandson of Kenya’s third president, Mwai Kibaki who died on April 22, 2022, at the age of 90.

His father and mother first met in high school and became friends for several years before getting into a relationship and deciding to marry.

Sean has two siblings Mwai Kibaki Jr and Emma Nagini from the same parents. Jimmy, a Kenyan businessman is 58 years old.

After finishing his education in Canada, he returned to Kenya in 1988 and began a company with a loan from his father. In 2009, Jimmy decided to launch Simama Kenya, which translates as “stand up Kenya.” Some regarded this as a political ploy.

According to a statement made by the national youth congress, the initiative was a phony political operation seeking to steal their aims. Throughout his career, he has earned a great deal of respect and renown for his work.

The news of late former President Mwai Kibaki’s death impacted his grandkids the hardest, and they are still trying to process it, according to Jimmy Kibaki’s wife.

“The grandkids were hurt the hardest since they are younger, but we will be able to bear the load and void that Mzee has left with time, she speak.

Mwai Kibaki Grandchildren, Sean Andrew Thanked Followers For The support

Mwai Kibaki’s grandsons, Sean Andrew and Mwai Kibaki Jr. paid tribute to their grandfather in a heart-touching way.

Kibaki Jr. expressed gratitude to the public for their support and referred to the late Kibaki as a legend.

“Sometimes, a legend that lasts for decades does so for a purpose.” Realizing the power of our dreams and inspiring galaxies of greatness for future generations.

Sean paid honor to his grandfather by replacing his profile photo on all of his social media accounts with a blackout photo.

Normally, utilizing a blackout profile photo is a form of social protest. However, other social media experts suggest that it frequently occurs when the person is depressed or sad over something.

Sean responded to all his fan’s support through his post. He wrote, “I’m overwhelmed by everyone’s generosity. From phone conversations, text messages, and direct messages, even when we meet.”

“It is your optimism that is getting us through this, and I sincerely thank you from the bottom of my heart,” he added.

He was quite close to both his paternal and maternal grandfathers. When he was 13, he lost his maternal grandfather.

Following his grandfather’s death, Sean confessed in an honest interview with Vivianne Wandera of EveWoman that he was tormented a lot in school. He didn’t have anybody to talk to and was almost abducted as a child.

“In the past, I’ve suffered from mental health concerns and depression.” I had lost individuals important to me, and the fact that I was bullied a lot contributed to my sadness.”

“When my maternal grandfather died when I was 13 years old, I suffered from depression, which became so severe that I contemplated suicide at one point, he stated.”

He also said that he was able to come out of it by “seeking counseling and doing soul-searching.”

Sean Andrew Kibaki’s Story As Jimmy Kibaki’s Son

Sean Andrew Kibaki, the son of Jimmy Kibaki and grandson of Mwai Kibaki is a graduate from United States International University (USIU).

He went viral when he lovingly eulogized his grandmother. He rose to prominence in May 2016 when he took to the stage to pay respect to his late grandmother and former First Lady Lucy Kibaki.

In a question-and-answer session on social media, he spoke up about his future plans, stating that he had previously had a no-strings-attached arrangement and will continue to do so in the future.

Andrew has also stated that he does not intend to have children in the future. This will undoubtedly disappoint many women who would like to build a family with the prominent model.

Sean Andrew is a passionate photographer and a fashion model signed by Art Mashariki headquartered in Nairobi. He is eager to succeed in a sector with enormous potential.

He is unquestionably destined for success.
