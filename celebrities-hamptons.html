10 Celebrities You're Almost Guaranteed To Spot In the Hamptons

Known for its pristine beaches, renowned restaurants, and magnificent estates, it is no surprise that the Hamptons are a celebrity hot spot. Located approximately 100 miles from New York City on the east end of Long Island, the Hamptons are a playground for the city’s rich and famous. With peak season in the Hamptons stretching from Memorial Day to Labor Day, summer renters can expect to pay up to $1 million dollars a month for a luxury estate! Therefore, it is no surprise that prime real estate frequently lists (and sells) for over $100 million dollars. While these prices may seem extreme, they make accessibility into the Hamptons ultra exclusive, and perfect for the elusive celebrity looking to spend some relaxing time at the beach.

From Oscar-winning actors at seaside hotels to supermodels at exclusive enclaves, these luxury Hamptons haunts are sure to be swimming with a-list celebrities all summer long. Now let's take a look at some of the most infamous Hamptons regulars and the hangouts they like to frequent.

Leonardo DiCaprio

Academy Award winning actor, Leonardo DiCaprio, is no stranger to the Hamptons, and can be spotted out and about at numerous places over the summer months. However, one spot in particular, The Surf Lodge, located on Fort Pond in Montauk, may be the best place to spot Leo in the wild.

Known for its exclusivity, packed parties, and coastal chic decor, The Surf Lodge is a frequented spot by many celebrities for a quick cocktail at the beach bar, a happening event on the sandy pond-front, or an overnight stay in the boutique hotel.

Beyoncé & Jay-Z

Back in 2017, it was reported that the buyers of the notable “Pond House” in East Hampton was none other than power-couple, Beyoncé & Jay-Z. The property is a secluded and idyllic escape from New York City, while remaining close to East Hampton’s busting Main Street and the hamlet of Amagansett. Just the year before, Beyoncé & Jay-Z were infamously spotted at Stephen Talk House, a popular music venue in Amagansett, for a last-minute private performance by the band Coldplay.

Beyoncé & Jay-Z, along with Blue, Sir, and Rumi in tow, can also be spotted enjoying an easy picnic on the beach during warm sunny days. The beach closest to the family’s compound is Georgia Beach in East Hampton, a beautiful and intimate spot for swimming, sunning, and taking long walks along the Atlantic Ocean.

Gwenyth Paltrow

Actress, lifestyle guru, and Hamptons regular, Gwenyth Paltrow can be seen everywhere from attending luxury local events to reading a book on the beach.

While her home is in Amagansett, one might be able to catch a peek of her having dinner at the popular Mediterranean restaurant Nick & Toni’s, located in East Hampton. If you’re extra lucky, she may just be dining out with other celebrity regulars at the restaurant like Jack Nicholson, Jessica Alba, and Jimmy Fallon.

Scarlett Johansson

A homeowner in Amagansett since 2014, actress Scarlett Johansson is a frequent sighting in the Hamptons. While she is commonly seen sunning on various Hamptons beaches, she has recently been spotted at Gurney’s Resort & Spa in Montauk.

The luxury resort has many particular features making it a celebrity go-to, such as an opulent spa, delectable Italian restaurant, and an ocean side bar and lounge.

Sarah Jessica Parker

While she may be best known for her leading role in the iconic HBO television series, Sex and the City, actress Sarah Jessica Parker is also know for her beachside getaway in the Hamptons.

Nearby to Sarah Jessica Parker and husband Matthew Broderick’s chic cottage in Amagansett, is the iconic Lobster Roll restaurant on Montauk Highway. Also known as “LUNCH” from it’s retro red, white, and blue sign, the seaside eatery is a common spot for the family when taking a break from the sun and surf.

Kelly Ripa

Television personality Kelly Ripa and husband Mark Consuelos spend almost every summer in the Hamptons with their full family at their cedar shake home in Southampton.

With the family’s repeated Summer stays, it is no surprise to spot Kelly Ripa at SoulCycle classes, cruising around Sag Harbor, or out to dinner with fellow celebrity pals. However, one spot in particular, Indian Wells Tavern in Amagansett, seems to be a favorite of the television star. The casual and pub-like spot is ideal for an easy lunch with friends, a cold drink on a hot summer’s day, or for grabbing a late night snack.

Billy Joel

Famed singer and songwriter Billy Joel was born and raised on Long Island. Therefore, it is no surprise that he has become a celebrity staple in the Hamptons. While currently working on a major renovation project at his new Sag Harbor home, he can often be seen boating through the Peconic Bay.

He has been known to make appearances across the bay at Claudio’s in Greenport on Long Island’s North Fork. The waterfront restaurant and bar is a lively space for cocktails, fresh seafood, live music, and sweeping views of the Peconic Bay.

Andy Cohen

Television personality, reality show producer, and pop culture guru Andy Cohen is a recent addition to the Hamptons celebrity regulars after setting down roots in a small seaside shack in Sag Harbor.

Just a few miles south of his cottage is Almond, a restaurant and bar in Bridgehampton, known for its Friday night get-togethers of A-list locals, Andy included. Now a father, Andy recently upsized his Hampton’s real estate portfolio by purchasing a waterfront property in Amagansett thanks to the help of fellow Hamptons celebrity regular and close friend, Sarah Jessica Parker.

Alec Baldwin

Another Long Island native, Alec Baldwin, along with wife Hilaria Baldiwn, spend most of their time at the family’s modern farmhouse in East Hampton. Alec has made note that he prefers the tranquility and relaxed vibe of the Hamptons over the hustle and bustle of New York Cit,y and can be observed at numerous east end shops, restaurants, and beaches.

One spot in particular, Bostwick’s Chowder House in East Hampton, is a low-key seasonal eatery close to the popular Two Mile Hollow Beach and a favorite spot for locals like Alec.

Christie Brinkley

Possibly the most likely celebrity to spot in the Hamptons may be legendary supermodel and Hamptons resident Christie Brinkley. While the stunning beauty recently sold her waterfront Sag Harbor home, she still holds onto her sprawling Bridgehampton estate. As a resident for most of the year, Christie can be spotted at local farmer’s markets, small charity events, and various beaches, even during the “off” seasons.

One hot-spot in particular, Sunset Beach on Shelter Island, is just a short ferry ride or boat trip from the Hamptons or the North Fork of Long Island and has hosted the blond bombshell a number of times. The boutique hotel sits right on the Peconic Bay and features a quaint luxury shop and a fantastic indoor/outdoor restaurant and multi-level bar. The easy waterfront access and pristine beach brings many celebrities looking for a fun day trip to the colorful and secluded hotel.
