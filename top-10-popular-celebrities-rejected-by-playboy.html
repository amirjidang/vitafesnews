One of America's favourite magazine is Playboy. The American magazine covers nude pictures of famous celebrities such as: Marilyn Monroe, Lindsay Lohan, Kim Kardashian and many more famous celebrities. However there is a list of celebrities that were denied by Playboy. Here is the list of few Celebrity who got rejected for the cover page of the famous magazine:

10.  Audrina Patridge

Audrina is a Reality TV Star, she started her career when her nude photos accidentally got leaked on the web. She hired a photographer to do a hot photo shoot for submission to Playboy. But the photos got rejected.

9.  T.a.t.u

The lesbian pop duo of Russia, T.a.t.u wanted to pose for Playboy on their 18th birthday, but got rejected from both American and German editions of the magazine.

8.  Bridget Marquardt

Bridget Marquardt is one of the "girls next door", but she could not get her pics in Playboy. The Buxom babe was rejected after some failed test shoots.

7.  Shirley Jones

Shirley Jones is now 79 years old. She played the role of a mom in "The Partridge Family". Shirley showed interest in posing for Playboy, She even did some test shots, but Hefner rejected the pictures because he expected more nudity from the actress.

 6.  Luann De Lesseps

"Real Housewives of New York city" starer Luann was visibly jealous, when her cast-mate "Kelly Bensimon" was asked to pose for Playboy. She sounded off with " no one asked me"!, She later retracted her interest by saying: " I think my 15 year old would have a heart attack, if I were to appear in Playboy".

5.  Kate Gosselin

Kate Gosselin has taken some tough blows from the magazine. According to Hefner, he does not think "She is a celebrity". That's the second time that Gosselin gets rejected by Playboy. However, Kate's ex-husband Jon got offered to pose for PLAYGIRL magazine for $20,000.

4.  Courtney Stodden

One day after her 18th birthday, the sexy teen Courtney tweeted "no to porn......yes to playboy". The magazine refuses to believe that her breasts are real and denied her photo-shoot.

3.  Kelly Osbourne

Kelly Osbourne expressed her interest in posing for Playboy. Unfortunately, Hefner didn't think that "she was Playboy Material". Hefner's response was incredibly harsh for Kelly.

2.  Farrah Abraham

The 22 years old Farrah Abraham was bound to try for Playboy. Farrah reached out for the magazine, but got rejected twice by Playboy after her sex tape "backdoor teen mom" got released.

1.  Britney Spears

She wasn't exactly rejected. Hefner said that "he is more than happy to feature Britney in the magazine, but once she can put herself back on the road to recovery". Playboy denied Britney, when the famous singer shaved her head in 2007. Now we cannot foresee the mother of two, stripping down anytime soon.
