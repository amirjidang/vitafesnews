WWE Live Event Results (8/6) Melbourne, Australia- Shawn Michaels Guest General Manager, NXT Title M

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 19, 2024

WWE Live Event Results: Melbourne, Australia

Guest GM Shawn Michaels came out and announced that the WWE Title match would be changed to a Steel Cage Match

Finn Balor defeated Neville in a NXT Title Match. Had lots of good spots. Both wrestled great.

Fandango defeated Adam Rose.
Probably the worst match of the night. Could’ve been better.

Luke Harper beat R Truth. Average match. Truth got the crowd into it at the start but it got boring real fast. 

Randy Orton beat King Barrett. Really entertaining match. Orton grabbed a kids Cena hat, put it on Barrett, taunted him with a You Can’t See Me and punched him.

Bo Dallas came out and did his usual bullcrap. Shawn Michaels came out. Bo called him a washed up has been and Shawn superkicked him.

Natalya and Emma beat The Bella. Pretty good match. Great to see Emma back in Australia. Emma got a massive pop.

Cesaro beat Kevin Owens . Both got amazing pop from the crowd and they put on the match of the night.

Seth Rollins beat Dean Ambrose in a Cage Match for the WWE Title. Really great match. Crowd was really into it. After Seth won, he pulled out a table and tried to put Dean through it but Dean reversed it and put Seth through it. 

Biggest Pops:
Emma 
Shawn Michaels
Kevin Owens

Biggest Heat:
King Barrett
Bo Dallas
Seth Rollins
