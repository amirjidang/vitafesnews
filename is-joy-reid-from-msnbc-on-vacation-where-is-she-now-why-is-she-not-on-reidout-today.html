Is Joy Reid From MSNBC On Vacation? Where Is She Now &amp; Why Is She Not On ReidOut Today?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Is Joy Reid from MSNBC on vacation? Fans are curious about the journalist’s present whereabouts after Tiffany Cross replaces her in the ReidOut.

Joy Reid is an American TV host, MSNBC national correspondent, political commentator, and author.

She is recognized as the host of The ReidOut program on MSNBC, a weeknight show about political scenarios and other discussions. Reid has been running the program since 2020 and is famous for her opinions and her style of journalism.

However, people didn’t see the woman in the recent episode of the program. Instead of Joy, Tiffany Cross hosted the program as the original host was nowhere to be seen during the whole program.

Her disappearance from the show has left the audience to ponder her situation as they are curious about the lady’s whereabouts. The news of her vacation has also spread on the internet. Here is everything to know about Reid’s absence from the TV program.

Is Joy Reid From MSNBC On Vacation?

Yes, the ReidOut host on MSNBC, Joy Reid, is on vacation in Paris.

The official Twitter page of the show confirmed that the lady was on vacation during the program’s premiere. It also revealed that Cross would fill in on Joy’s absence while urging the viewers to tune in for the regular episodes.

From this notice, we can confirm that Reid is currently enjoying her vacation. Although the message didn’t mention where the journalist was having a good time, we can trace her location in Paris based on her recent Instagram posts.

We are now unaware when the lady will return to her job and reappear on ReidOut as there are no official statements in this regard.

Where Is Joy Reid Today? Update Now

The American television host Joy Reid is currently in Paris, France, enjoying her vacation.

Reid’s today’s location is untraceable since she has not disclosed the places she would visit during her trip to the outsiders. She posted pictures on Instagram six days ago, indicating her location in Paris.

From this, we can confirm that the woman is in France for her vacation, but the question of whether she will visit other cities in the country remains unknown. As a result, we cannot pinpoint her exact location.

Joy Reid Salary And Net Worth

With an annual salary of $1.5 million, Joy Reid has a net worth of $4 million, as per Celebrity Net Worth.

Her salary figure is her annual revenue from her engagement in journalism, where she mainly contributes to MSNBC. Besides her reporting income, the lady also makes a good amount of money from the royalty income of her books.

Considering her multiple income streams, there is no doubt that the woman is a multi-millionaire with a couple of millions at her disposal.

Who Is Joy Reid Husband?

Joy Reid is with her husband, Jason Reid, a film producer, writer, editor, and freelancer.

Despite his active involvement in multiple fields, Jason is better known as Joy’s spouse. They tied the knot in 1997 and have been together for around 25 years. Nonetheless, the background story of their love life, such as how and when they met, remains unknown to the outsiders.

Joy and Jason have three children, two sons and a daughter. They are Winsome Reid, Jmar Reid, and Nasir Reid.
