Cathriona White (born 14 September 1985) was a famous Irish actress and make-up artist from Cappawhite, Ireland. She was well-known in the entertainment industry as the girlfriend of the famous actor Jim Carrey.

Both of them were dating from the year 2012. She is famous in the public for her best performance in the movie  Bunker 731, Firefly, and The Online Gamer.

She loves Jim Carrey very much. Jim and her relationship had been going on since 2012. According to sources, Carrey dated White for only a few months and later broke up with her.

The media says that White committed suicide after some time of the third death anniversary of her father.
