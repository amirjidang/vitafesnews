Heres Why Below Deck Fans Think Jean-Luc Is Dani Soares Baby Daddy

Posted by Patria Henriques on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Below Deck has had some crazy moments out on the sea, however, the series newest spin-off, Below Deck: Sailing Yacht, is shaping up to be quite the season.

With a handful of new cast members, including Dani Soares, Alli Dore, Colin Macrae, and Jean-Luc Lanaux, to name a few. As the drama began episode one, there are also a handful of crew romances that are on full display.

While Gary and Alli are starting their romance out, things between Dani and Jean-Luc have been steaming up. Well, after posting a pregnant photo of herself on Instagram, Dani has decided to keep the identity of the baby's father secret, leaving many to wonder if Jean-Luc could be the baby daddy.

Is Jean-Luc Dani's Baby Daddy?

When it comes to Below Deck, there is never any shortage of nights off, cocktails, drama, and of course, hookups, and the second season of Sailing Yacht is certainly no exception.

As fans were introduced to a brand new all-single cast this time around, they knew that they would be in for quite the bumpy ride, and bump it has been. Whether the Parsifal III is hitting docks, or romances hitting rocky waters, there is always something going on.

When it comes to the boatmances this time around, there have been a few flirty moments that have viewers wondering whether or not the crew will take things to the next level. Although Gary, Alli, and Sydney found themselves in a love triangle, all eyes are now on Danie Soares and Jean-Luc.

The two finally shared a kiss in last week's episode, sparking interest in whether the two will take things farther. During an interview on Watch What Happens Live, Dani revealed that she was really only in it for the flirt.

In addition to revealing that, Dani also let fans in on the fact that she is expecting her first child! The star posted a selfie of herself back in April showing off her growing baby bump. "It's just you and me" she captioned the photo.

Related: Exclusive: Natasha De Bourg Of 'Below Deck' Reveals How She Became 'The Classy Chef'

It's become clear that Dani is not interested in revealing the identity of who the baby daddy is, and rightfully so! Despite her wishes, fans are throwing around names that could possibly be it, including Jean-Luc.

Considering the two do not follow one another nor tag each other in group photos on Instagram, viewers are certain that things did not end well between the two. As if that weren't bad enough, it appears as if charter guest, Barrie-Drewitt Barlow also spilled the beans on the matter.

During an Instagram Live video, Barlow revealed that "He [Jean-Luc] was too busy getting the other one pregnant," when asked about his thoughts on this season's crew. Yikes!

While Dani nor Jean-Luc have confirmed or denied the rumors, fans are now flooding Jean-Luc's socials with comments about the topic at hand. Jean-Luc made it clear that their romance does, in fact, blossom on the show, so viewers are looking forward to seeing where things go from here.
