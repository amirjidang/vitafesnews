Matriarch Mothra Controversy &amp; Allegations, How Was Ronnie Radke Involved?

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Was Ronnie Radke involved in the Matriarch Mothra controversy? He is making headlines with his TikTok comeback.

Radke is an American singer, rapper, musician, songwriter, and record producer who started his career in 1998. He is the former lead singer of Escape the Fate and the current lead singer of Falling in Reverse.

Ronnie is a convicted felon who has served jail time for several crimes. He rose to fame with Escape the Fate but got kicked out due to a prison sentence in 2008. He began his new band, Falling in Reverse, after his release.

Matriarch Mothra Controversy & Allegations

Matriarch Mothra, aka Social Repose, is an American singer-songwriter and YouTube personality. He has landed himself in controversy several times. He was once outed for purposefully putting women through abuse for his music and art.

Mothra was born on February 9, 1990, and his current age is 32 years. He started his career in 2011, and his genre includes Indie pop, art pop, Avant pop, acapella, experimental, electronic. His full name is Richard Mclean Giese.

Matriarch wears a distinct outfit that includes face paint, mechanical wings, and a native American war bonnet resembling a headdress. He keeps adding new components to his outfit, and most of them are custom-made.

He describes his style as “alternative.” Further details about Mothra’s controversy and allegations are yet to be revealed to the media. Fans might find out more about the incident once he decides to open up about them on his social media.

Some people are not understanding how Ronnie has that hold on so many people.
