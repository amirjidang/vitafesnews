Keeping Up With The Smiths: Inside The Fortunes Of Will Smiths Family

Will Smith’s career is currently falling to pieces following the controversy that happened during the Academy Awards ceremony. Although his career is now in shambles, it is undeniable that he has built massive wealth over the course of his career as well as his entire family. Since he has risen to fame in the 90s starring the series The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air, he has seen achieved so much in his career.

With Will Smith's fame, his whole family has made the most of his fame and gained success and personal wealth from it as well. Though the Smith patriarch is obviously the wealthiest of them all, the kids have already earned millions as well. Take a look inside the wealth of Will Smith’s family members.

Trey Smith

https://www.pinterest.ph/pin/33003009755293145/

American actor Willard Christopher Smith III shares the same name as his father Will Smith. He is the first child of Will Smith and is the only child he had with his first wife, actress Sheree Zampino. The actor who was born on November 11, 1992 in Los Angeles has an estimated net worth of $2 million. The ex couple were only married for three years from 1992 until 1995. Trey Smith is an actor and DJ and has starred in numerous projects including America’s Next Top Model, All of Us and the music video for AcE: Find You Somewhere sang by his siblings Jaden and Willow.

Willow Smith

https://www.pinterest.ph/pin/400257485640532934/

Willow Camille Reign Smith is the youngest child and only daughter of Will Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith. The American singer and actress was born on October 31, 2000 in Los Angeles, California. Willow Smith has received numerous awards and recognition throughout her music career including NAACP Image Award, BET Award, and Young Artist Award. She was also nominated for an MTV Video Music Award and Daytime Emmy Award. The singer currently has an estimated net worth of $6 million thanks to her successful music career. She made her first appearance in the big screen in the film I Am Legend starring with her father in 2007. She entered the music industry with her single Whip My Hair which was a platinum-certified single that peaked at number 11 on Billboard Hot 100. Although her parents already had so much wealth and she can basically ride on their fame, Willow made sure to make it on her own and established a music career basing on her talent.

Jaden Smith

https://www.pinterest.ph/pin/42291683989776710/

American rapper, singer and actor Jaden Christopher Syre Smith who is widely known as Jaden Smith is the second child of Will Smith, and the first child of Jada and Will Smith. The rapper has received numerous recognition and awards including MTV Movie Award, YouTube Creator Award, Young Artist Award and BET Award. He has been recognized and nominated for the Grammy Awards, Empire Awards and two NAACP Image Awards. His current net worth is $8 million which he earned primarily from his YouTube channel and music releases. He has started his career at a very young age upon starring with his father Will Smith in the film The Pursuit of Happyness. He has quickly branched out into his own path outside the shadows of his father as a solo artist thanks to his musical talent.

Jada Pinkett Smith

https://www.pinterest.ph/pin/600315825341990116/

American actress, producer, director, singer, author and businesswoman Jada Pinkett Smith earned her wealth through her acting career in TV and films. She currently has a net worth of $50 million although reports suggest that a large portion of his wealth came from his marriage from Will Smith who was basically among the highest paid actors for the past two decades. The 50 year old actress who has been struggling with Alopecia since 2018 is best known for her roles in films such as The Matrix Reloaded, The Matrix Resurrections, The Matrix Revolutions and the sitcom A Different World.

Will Smith

https://www.pinterest.ph/pin/630433647834541723/

American actor, rapper, and producer Willard Carroll Smith II is among the most bankable stars in Hollywood today. The actor who was born on September 25, 1968 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania has received numerous awards and recognition throughout the course of his career. He is noted for his works in both TV and movie. He has received awards for his performances including BAFTA Award, Golden Globe Award, Academy Award, four Grammy Awards and Screen Actors Guild Award. He has also been nominated for a Tony Award and a Primetime Emmy Award. He is among the few black individuals who have been nominated for Grammy, Oscar, Emmy and Tony Awards. He has been in the industry for almost four decades now and has achieved so much in his career. He reportedly earns at least $40 million from film projects and different endeavors annually although some say that he even bought home a whooping $80 million in a single year. He currently has an estimated net worth of $350 million. He made his wealth primarily from his acting career.
