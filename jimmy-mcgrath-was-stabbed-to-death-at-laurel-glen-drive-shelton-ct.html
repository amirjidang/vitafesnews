Jimmy Mcgrath Was Stabbed To Death At Laurel Glen Drive Shelton Ct

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Jimmy Mcgrath was stabbed to death in a stabbing incident at Laurel Glen Drive, Connecticut. Other three teenagers have been reported wounded. 

Jimmy Mcgrath, a 17-year-old Fairfield Prep student, has died in a stabbing incident that happened between students in Connecticut.

According to the reports published by nbcconnecticut.com, no arrest has been made yet, and the investigation is underway.

Obituary: Jimmy Mcgrath Was Stabbed To Death At Laurel Glen Drive Shelton Ct.

Jimmy Mcgrath has died in the stabbing fight that happened between teenage students at Laurel Glen Drive, Shelton, Connecticut. This is not an official obituary.

Three people were stabbed, and one of them, a 17-year-old male who has been reported dead as Jimmy Mcgrath, according to Shelton police.

After a fighting and stabbing report, officers were dispatched just before midnight Saturday.

When the police officers arrived on the scene, they discovered a large group of teenagers outside a home on Laurel Glen Drive.

According to police, four stabbing victims were found and treated right away. A 17-year-old male was later pronounced dead as one of the victims.

The school issued a statement saying, ” “The Fairfield Prep community mourns the untimely death of one of our juniors with great sadness. We are keeping him, his family, and friends in our thoughts and prayers as the school responds to our suffering community.”

Shelton police were informed around 11:55 p.m. to Laurel Glen Drive on reports of a fight and stabbing by the locals.

What Happened To Jimmy Mcgrath?

A 17-year-old Jimmy Mcgrath was a victim of the stabbing incident and three other teenagers. The other three teenagers survived, whereas Jimmy was pronounced dead despite giving emergency assistance at the location.

There have been no arrests made on the stabbing incident to date, and an investigation is going on while the parents mourn the loss of a young soul in the unfortunate incident.

Fairfield Prep later confirmed the 17-year-status old’s as a junior student from the school. Police officials have urged anyone with information to contact the Detective Bureau at 203-924-1544.

A similar double stabbing incident was reported at the University of Connecticut apartment complex in February 2022.

Around 11 p.m. Saturday, UConn Police responded to a call of a fight with injuries at the University of Connecticut campus.

They discovered two people with “superficial” stab wounds when they arrived. Two injured people received stitches and other minor injuries at Windham Hospital before being released.
