Benzino Shot At His Mothers Funeral In Massachusetts

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Rapper Benzino was shot multiple times today (Mar. 29) at his mother’s funeral in Duxbury, Massachusetts after a family feud came to a head at the unfortunate gathering. Reporter at 7News Adam Harding broke the story via Twitter, stating that Benzino was shot by his 36 year-old nephew, Gai Scott, with whom he had a previously standing beef. The Love & Hip-Hop reality star is expected to survive the shooting and is in stable condition. Police have arrest Scott and charged him with assault with the intent to murder.
