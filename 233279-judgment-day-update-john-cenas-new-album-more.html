Judgment Day Update, John Cenas New Album, More

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 5, 2024


Source: PWInsider

Thanks to Garrett Gonzales for the following:
Being that I’m a hip hop fan and a John Cena fan, I went out looking for the Cena CD. Looks like some of the stores like Best Buy and Circuit City are taking a “wait and see” attitude with the CD as they didn’t order many copies. Best Buy in San Jose, CA only ordered 10 and was still waiting for them while Circuit City sold the only two they received. However, because of the great service that is ITunes, I was able to buy and download the entire album and have posted a review to it. The album from a hip hop standpoint is actually decent, and from the perspective of someone so new to the game as Cena putting out a full CD, it’s pretty impressive. You can read it on the consumer review site Epinions.com here: http://www.epinions.com/content_182858911364.

Brian Bergeson sent this one; I was going to go to the Judgment Day Meet & Greet. I just got this in email about it. “We are in receipt of your request to purchase tickets to the Meet & Greet Session with SmackDown! Superstars; unfortunately, this session has been canceled due to lack of interest. Your money order payment has been mailed back to you. We regret any inconvenience.”

Judd B. sent this one; WWE has set up a webpage dedicated to John Cena’s brand new album which have hit stores. On it, you can download songs onto your Ipod, buy the CD, listen to Cena’s guest appearance on “Byte This” this past week and more.
