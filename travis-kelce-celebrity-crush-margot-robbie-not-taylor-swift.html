Travis Kelce Said This Blonde Beauty Was His Celebrity Crush And It Wasn't Taylor Swift

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce have faced controversy in their relationship, with Kelce's ex-girlfriends accusing him of cheating and being stingy.
  • Travis Kelce named Margot Robbie as his ultimate celebrity crush, not Taylor Swift, in a resurfaced interview.
  • Taylor Swift and Margot Robbie have a friendly and working relationship.

Since musician Taylor Swift and football player Travis Kelce made their relationship official, they have become one of the most famous couples in entertainment and sports. Despite their swoon-worthy courtship, which began when Kelce publicly confessed his interest in Swift in July 2023, their relationship has been surrounded by controversy. For example, two of Kelce's ex-girlfriends, Maya Benberry and Kayla Nicole, have condemned his previous actions, with Benberry saying he cheated on her and Nicole saying he was stingy regarding money.

Swift has also come under fire from critics who believe she has moved on too fast from her previous relationships with embattled rock star Matt Healy and actor Joe Alwyn. However, despite the criticism, Swift and Kelce have weathered the storm. But that hasn't stopped people from digging up any information they can find about the two, including a resurfaced interview in which Kelce named his ultimate celebrity crush, and it wasn't Taylor Swift!

Travis Kelce Admitted A Famous Actress Was His Celebrity Crush

While Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift's romance is in the beginning stages, in 2016, the football player was a young bachelor looking for love on the E! Reality TV dating show Catching Kelce.

In the series, fifty eligible ladies from across the country competed against each other to win the heart of the Kansas City Chiefs tight end.

Ultimately, social media influencer Maya Benberry won the series and dated Kelce. Sadly, their relationship lasted only a few months after the show finished filming after Benberry alleges she discovered Kelce was seeing other women behind her back.

While Kelce's fraught relationship with Benberry has stirred up controversy, fans and the media have since examined Travis' behavior on the show and have related it to his current relationship with Swift.

A case in point is when Kelce was doing promotional interviews for his reality series, and he was asked who his ultimate celebrity crush was...

In the question-and-answer video for E!, Travis was first asked what trait in a woman turned him off the most. The football player said he didn't like women who were stuck up and into themselves.

He was also asked what traits he wanted in a woman. He said that she had to be funny. He also answered that he preferred a woman who had a natural appearance and that he didn't care much for makeup.

Kelce also said that he preferred a woman to wear heels instead of sneakers because he liked them to show off their legs. When asked if he liked beauty or brains, Kelce replied that he wanted his girlfriend to have both.

Finally, when asked who his celebrity crush was, Travis Kelce answered Australian actress Margot Robbie and then blew a kiss at the camera.

Fans have also dug up an interview on TikTok posted by the sports drink Accelerator, for which Kelce was a brand ambassador, where Kelce was asked again who his celebrity crush was. Once again, Kelce stated Margot Robbie and added British singer Dua Lipa. Travis then commented that although he found them attractive, he knew he didn't stand a chance with either.

Given how Travis used the media to gain Swift's attention, the football player has recently been accused of trying to court Margot Robbie and Dua Lipa similarly. However, Kelce has not responded to these allegations and likely never will.

Is Taylor Swift On Good Terms With Margot Robbie?

Taylor Swift has many celebrity friends, including Selena Gomez, Lena Dunham, Cara Delevingne, Gigi Hadid, and Sofie Turner, and the list continues. While the songstress prides herself on supporting and uplifting other women, it's perhaps not surprising that she is on equally friendly terms with Margot Robbie.

The two blonde beauties also have an excellent working relationship. In 2022, they starred in the comedy-mystery film Amsterdam. Amsterdam tells the story of friends who witness a gruesome crime in which they become the subject of a police probe.

In a 2022 interview with Capital FM, Margot Robbie briefly discussed her relationship with Taylor Swift.

This interview has gained traction recently, given Swift's romantic relationship with Kelce and for his previous declarations of love to Margot Robbie.

Robbie stated in the interview that Swift once gave her a vinyl copy of her best-selling album "Folklore" and other merchandise.

She added that when she took it home with her, all of her friends freaked out over the gesture from Taylor Swift. She said that she had to give most of her merchandise away because her friends practically begged her for it.
