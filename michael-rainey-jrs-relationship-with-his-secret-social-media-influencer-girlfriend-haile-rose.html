The Truth About Michael Rainey Jr.'s Relationship With His Secret Social Media Influencer Girlfriend

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Michael Rainey Jr. had a career before Power, but the TV series is definitely his most famous project. Rainey Jr. has a net worth of $56 million and since he's only 22, that number is sure to only grow. A TV series can be a very lucrative way to make a living. The actor not only won a great role on Power, but he has starred in the show's two spinoffs.

With an Instagram following that is getting bigger all the time, many are watching Michael Rainey Jr. to see what he does next. With the news that he is in a relationship with influencer Haile Rose, fans are eager to find out more about how he spends his time when he isn't filming Power. When did Rainey Jr. begin dating Rose? Is there a specific reason why they don't talk about their relationship?

What Is Michael Rainey Jr. And Haile Rose's Relationship Like?

While some influencers have other careers, Haile Rose seems to be sticking to social media for now. Since she's in her early 20s, she has lots of time to decide if she wants to continue being an influencer or go another way. Earlier in 2023, several websites began talking about Rose's romance with Michael Rainey Jr.

Distractify.com reported on Michael Rainey Jr. and Haile Rose's relationship in March 2023. The website noted that the timeline of their romance is unknown. They have been active on each other's social media accounts since September 2022, though.

Rose shared a video of the two of them when he turned 22. She wrote “City girls down 1000" in the caption and he said. “You up 10K.”

While Michael Rainey Jr. and Haile Rose are both very active on Instagram, they haven't posted pictures with one another. Even though they have been dating for a while, they seem to be avoiding discussing each other online.

Rainey Jr. posts memes and news about Power on his account. And Rose posts many selfies and often shares what she's wearing. Sometimes, she lets fans learn more about her life.

In May 2023, she posted a photo of herself and wrote in the caption, "A very interesting past two weeks. Last selfie of the year. 2 different countries in the last two weeks. Applying for my international drivers license. Learning Japanese and Italian at the sameee time."

While the couple isn't talking about their current relationship, Rainey Jr. has chatted about his perspective on dating in the past.

Rainey Jr. discussed dating with Galore Mag in 2016. He said that he didn't want someone to wear a lot of beauty products. He said, "A deal breaker for me is too much makeup all the time. That’s a no-no. I could understand if it was for an event or something, but too much all the time is definitely a no-no for me."

The actor also answered a question about showing someone that he wanted to date them. He explained, "I guess just the way I talk to her, and I am a little more shy with her than if she was just a girl that was my friend. For me it’s easier if she kinda is into me first, then it makes my job easier."

No information is available about Haile Rose's previous relationships. Michael Rainey Jr. Before dating Rose, Rainey Jr. was linked to Paige Hurd, who also stars on Power Book II: Ghost. He also dated a model named Eva Apio.

When the actor is interviewed, he is always asked about his Power character and any interesting storylines. It's possible that if he was asked about his girlfriend, it would be honest. But when he talks to the media, the focus isn't often on his personal life.

What Are Michael Rainey Jr. And Haile Rose Known For?

Michael Rainey Jr. played Orange Is The New Black as Michael Burset before he was cast as Tariq St. Patrick on the TV drama Power. He currently plays the same role on the spinoff Power Book II: Ghost and also starred as the same character in one episode of Power Book IV: Force.

Rainey Jr. and Haile Rose are both young stars who seem to really like what they do. That makes them a great couple.

Rainey Jr. then began playing Tariq St. Patrick on the TV drama Power. Courtney A. Kemp is the creator of Power, but 50 Cent also works on it. Rainey Jr. talked about his background when he was interviewed as the cover star of Cassius in early 2023. He shared that he got some helpful advice about acting from 50 Cent.

The actor said, "I feel like I’m in this for just the enjoyment. I like doing what I do. I like playing the role of Tariq.." He continued, "50 [Cent] always told me, “You can’t bring the awards to the bank, so it don’t matter.” The first time he told me that, I was like, I could never really care or go too crazy about not being seen for some of these awards, so yeah. I definitely just do it for my enjoyment. So whatever followers, whatever comes with it, comes with it, but if it doesn’t come with it, it is what it is."
