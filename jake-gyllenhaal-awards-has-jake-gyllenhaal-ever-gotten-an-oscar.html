Jake Gyllenhaal Awards: Has Jake Gyllenhaal ever gotten an Oscar?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 10, 2024

American actor, Jake Gyllenhaal does not have an Oscar yet, however, he was nominated for Academy Awards Best Supporting Actor with his movie Brokeback Mountain in 2006 but he lost it.

Jake Gyllenhaal awards

Has Jake Gyllenhaal won any awards in his career? What are some of the popular awards won by Jake Gyllenhaal? Jake Gyllenhaal has won a few awards in his career. Some of these awards include;

  • MTV Movie & TV Award for Best Kiss.
  • BAFTA Award for Best Actor in a Supporting Role.
  • Hollywood Actor Award.
  • Chlotrudis Award for Best Actor.
  • MTV Movie Award for Best Make Performance.
  • Independent Spirit Award for Best First Feature.

Jake Gyllenhaal movies and tv shows

What are some of the best movies and tv shows by Jake Gyllenhaal? Jake Gyllenhaal is known for several movies and tv shows. Some of them include;

  • Nightcrawler.
  • Ambulance.
  • Spider-Man: Far From Home.
  • Road House.
  • Love & Other Drugs.
  • Man vs. Wild.
  • Inside Amy Schumer.
