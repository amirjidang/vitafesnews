Comics and graphics have their loyal fanbase. They now have the scope to turn into a movie franchise, depending on their popularity. The writers of favorite comic collections have a jaw-dropping net worth simply through their successful writing careers.

Raina Telgemeier

Raina Telgemeier is one of the famous American comic book writers in the USA and has written several comics. She holds a fantastic reputation for breaking records and winning prestigious accolades for her work, as stated by Quora.

The talented writer’s works include Smile (2010), Drama (2012), Sisters (2014), Ghosts (2016), and Guts (2019). Raina has a net worth of approximately $1.5 million.

Jim Zub

Jim Zubkavich or Jim Zub is a Canadian comic book writer known for his Samurai Jack comics. He is also an art instructor.

The most notable works by Jim would include Skull-kickers, Wayward, Glitterbomb, Samurai Jack, and the Thunderbolts. Apart from these, Zub has also mastered comics like Dungeons & Dragons, Uncanny Avengers, and Avengers: No Road Home. These masterpieces have procured him a net worth of about $2.10 million.

Joe Quesada

Joe Quesada has earned fame as a comic book writer, editor, and TV producer. He was well known in 1990 for his Valiant comics, followed by the legendary Marvel and DC.

Joe’s renowned world consists of Ninjak and Solar, Man of the Atom, and Batman: Sword of Azrael and X-Factor under Marvel and DC comics. He is also famous for creating the character of Ash. Quesada has an impressive net worth of $10 million, according to ZUU Online.

Neil Gaiman

Neil Richard Gaiman is an English author and has earned considerable fame as a comic book writer. Gaiman is known for The Sandman, Neverwhere, American Gods, Stardust, Coraline, The Graveyard Book, Good Omens, and The Ocean at the End of the Lane.

Neil has also received several literary awards like the Hugo, Nebula, and Bram Stoker awards, to name a few. Gaiman currently boasts an impressive net worth of about $20 million.

Kevin Eastman and Peter Laird

Kevin Eastman and Peter Laird have net worth of approximately $20 million each through their successful authorship. They are the writers of the favorite Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles series.

The comic book industry is a lot about contacts and exposure. Peter Laird did a brilliant job in granting their works some exposure to the press and the TV, and soon, TMNT became one of the best-selling comic books.

Robert Kirkman

Robert is a brilliant American comic book writer who is also a letterer, editor, and executive producer. He is most famous for co-authoring comics like The Walking Dead, Invincible, Tech Jacket, and Outcast.

Among other notable works by Kirkman are Battle Pope, Marvel Zombies, Oblivion Song, and Irredeemable Ant-Man. He has a brilliant net worth of about $23 million.

Naoko Takeuchi

Naoko Togashi is known by her maiden name Naoko Takeuchi in the world of literary comics. She is one of the most famous and successful Japanese Manga writers.

She is best known for works like Sailor Moon and Codename: Sailor V. According to Yahoo! Finance, she has a fantastic net worth of about $33 million.

Akira Toriyama

Japanese manga writer Akira Toriyama is particularly popular for works like Dragon Ball, Dr. Slump, Dragon Quest, Chrono Trigger, and Blue Dragon. Among these, the writer has worked as the character designer for Dragon Quest and Chrono Trigger.

Toriyama is known to have sold over 350 million copies of his excellent works. Thus, it comes as no surprise that he has a significant net worth of about $50 million.

Stan Lee

Stan Lee is one of the popular names in the world of comics. Sadly expired in 2018, and the world grieved; however, this legend left behind a legacy.

Lee was an American comic book writer besides being a producer and editor. He is the mastermind behind Spider-Man, X-Men, Iron Man, Thor, Black Panther, Dr. Strange, and Ant-Man. As stated by Wealthy Genius, Stan had been one of the wealthiest comic book writers with an excellent net worth of $80 million.

David Choe

David Choe has had a career in writing, journalism, and music. He is best known for his work titled Slow Jams that he self-published and then donated at a 1998 Comic-Con. Choe is one of the wealthiest comic book writers with an extraordinary net worth of about $200 million.

Comic book writers have worked hard to reach this level of popularity and exposure, and today the book fandom is as large as that of literary novels or movies.
