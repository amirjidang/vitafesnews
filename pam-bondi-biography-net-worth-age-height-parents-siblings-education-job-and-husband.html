Pam Bondi Biography; Net Worth, Age, Height, Parents, Siblings, Education, Job And Husband

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 23, 2024

Pam Bondi is a famous American attorney, lobbyist, and politician who American served as the 37th Florida attorney general from 2011 to 2019.

Pam Bondi was born on November 17, 1965, in Tampa, Florida, in the United States of America. She is the daughter of Joseph and Pasty Bondi. She has two siblings; Beth Bondi McIntyre and Bradley J. Bondi.

Pam Bondi started her education at the C. Leon King High School and then proceeded to the University of Florida, where she graduated with a degree in Criminal Justice. Later on, attended the Stetson Law School, where she attained a Juris Doctor degree and graduated in 1990.
