Jerry Lawler &amp; Terry Funk Announced For An Empty Arena Retrospective At Starrcast II

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Jerry Lawler & Terry Funk Announced For A Starrcast II Event

The Starrcast Events Twitter handle just announced that WWE Hall of Famers Jerry Lawler and Terry Funk will be featured at Starrcast II in an “Empty Arena Retrospective” in which the two legends will recount their famous empty arena match that took place in Memphis, TN on April 13, 1981.

Many fans have wanted to hear the story behind this match…now at Starrcast II, they can hear it live!
