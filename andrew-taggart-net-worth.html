The Formation of The Chainsmokers

The Chainsmokers’ discography showcases their evolution as artists, featuring a captivating blend of EDM, pop, and alternative elements. Their ability to connect with their audience through relatable lyrics and infectious melodies has been a key factor in their enduring success.
