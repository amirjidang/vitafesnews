Report: WWE To Double-Tape SmackDown On 5/19

Posted by Kelle Repass on Monday, August 12, 2024

The May 26 episode of WWE SmackDown will reportedly be pre-taped.

Per Mike Johnson of PWInsider, the May 26 episode of WWE SmackDown will be taped on Friday, May 19. WWE will hold the May 19 taping of SmackDown at the Colonial Life Arena in Columbia, SC, and the event will be a double taping. The company will hold the Night of Champions premium live event in Saudi Arabia on May 27, so talent, staff, and crew will be traveling ahead of the show.

Undisputed WWE Universal Champion Roman Reigns is set to appear on the May 19 episode of SmackDown. Check out the card here.
