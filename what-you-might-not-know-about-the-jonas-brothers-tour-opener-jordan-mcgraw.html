What You Might Not Know About The Jonas Brothers Tour Opener, Jordan McGraw

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Jordan McGraw is a budding singer and guitarist who has opened for the Jonas Brothers on two tours now- Happiness Begins Tour, where he joined Bebe Rexha and Remember This Tour, where he opened before Kelsea Ballerini and other independent artists.

Despite him earning recognition from opening for the band, not too many people know who he is or much about him at all. Kevin, Joe and Nick Jonas have all become really close friends with McGraw and even celebrated his 35th birthday on the road on October 21. He was born in Texas in 1986.

He just released a new song and is on the verge of becoming a popular singer, judging by the crowd cheers on this tour. Here's what you might not know about the Jonas Brothers' tour opener, Jordan McGraw, including his relation to another celebrity and the advice the brothers have given him.

8 Who Jordan McGraw Is Related To

Perhaps the most note-worthy fact to mention about Jordan McGraw is that he is the son of TV host and author, Dr. Phil McGraw. His mom is Dr. Phil's current wife, Robin Jo Jameson. Jordan's brother, Jay, followed in his father's footsteps and went into the TV side of entertainment. He has written numerous books for young people and is the CEO of Stage 29 Productions. Jay also serves as executive producer on his dad show, Dr. Phil. Jordan is gaining more fame, but in the music aspect.

7 Music Career

McGraw learned to play guitar when he was a teenager and formed his first band in 2005 called The Upside. Sadly, that band was short-lived. In 2010, he joined Organica Music Group, which led him to perform as the lead guitarist for their band, Stars in Stereo. They disbanded in 2015.

After Stars in Stereo disbanded, he became the lead singer and guitarist of the band, Hundred Handed. The group consists of McGraw, Drew Langan (drums) and Matt Black (bass guitar). They band got their name from the creature of Greek and Roman Mythology, the Hundred Handed One. They gained traction when they released the song, "Love Me Like The Weekend." The band eventually broke up and went their own ways, paving the way for McGraw to start his solo career.

6 Jordan McGraw's Touring And Music

Jordan McGraw's career started to really take off when he was announced as an opener for the Jonas Brothers Happiness Begins Tour in 2019, where he played alongside Rexha. His song with Sarah Hyland, "Met At A Party" was very popular at the time, after releasing his self-titled EP in November of that year and establishing himself as an artist. His musical style would be a mix of Country, Pop Punk, Pop and EDM. “I’m a nineties kid making eighties music in 2021,” he told American Songwriter.

Now, touring again with the brothers and Ballerini on the Remember This tour, the fans seem to be more familiar with him, which makes him seem more comfortable on stage.

5 Advice Jordan McGraw Got From The Jonas Brothers

On a radio interview in 2019, McGraw revealed that Kevin, Nick and Joe Jonas are his best friends. Luckily, Joe and Kevin are also good advice givers. Being fathers themselves, they gave McGraw advice, as he has one child and another on the way. "Kevin's kids are a little older, so he's like king dad. He really sets the bar high and sets the example. And Joe's seven months ahead, so I get the immediate advice from Joe, and then I get the bigger, broader advice from Kevin. They've been a really awesome sounding board for, 'Oh, how do I handle this?" McGraw told PEOPLE.

Joe's daughter and McGraw's daughter has met up for playdates and have more planned for the future. They are besties in the making.

4 His Relationships

McGraw has been in a lot of relationships in the past including Playboy model, Crystal Harris and musician Marissa Jack, whom he dated for five years. He also dated The Bold And The Beautiful actress, Ragan Wallake. However, the musician got married to The Real Housewvives of Beverly Hills star, Morgan Stewart in December 2020. They dated 11 years ago and reconnected when McGraw was on tour in 2019 and now have an adorable family together. She was seven months pregnant at the time and their wedding was immediate family only.

3 Jordan McGraw's Children

McGraw and Stewart welcomed their first child, a daughter named Row Renggli in February 2021 and now they are expecting another little one, which they announced in September. The couple will celebrate their first wedding anniversary this December. Whenever they can, his wife and daughter join him on tour and Facetime when they can't make it, but McGraw told PEOPLE, he can't wait until his daughter can experience her first concert.

2 His New Song

McGraw released his new song "McConaughey," just this month after fans heard it on the road and wanted an official release. Being from Texas, he has always been a fan of Matthew McConaughey and even included the actors iconic, "Hey, hey, hey!" in the song. He has not met the actor yet and hopes he loves the song but did get an email saying 'McConaughey loves 'McConaughey,'' according to an interview with Entertainment Tonight!

1 Jordan McGraw's Net Worth

In 2018, outlets reported that his new worth was around $3 million. In just three years, it has grown substantially. Celebrity Net Worth puts the singer at a $20 million net worth in 2021. Along with his music career and touring, McGraw has also earned his money from selling his Beverly Hills home for $5.75 million. He currently lives in a $10 million Los Angeles mansion, that his parents gifted him after he and Stewart got engaged.
