Jason Momoas Closest Celebrity Friends

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Jason Momoa is super popular in the Hollywood industry because he has a great sense of humor, he’s learned some epic roles, and he is extremely handsome! He is one good-looking guy. Some of his biggest rules to date include HBO’s Game of Thrones, DC’s Aquaman, and Baywatch: Hawaii back in the early 2000s alongside David Hasselhoff.

Jason Momoa is naturally likable because he is such an amazing guy. It is obvious that he has a great personality based on the way he responds and interviews and how he reacts to others on red carpets. Here are some of his closest famous friends.

10 Camila Cabello

Camila Cabello absolutely freaked out the day that she met Jason Momoa. She realized she was a huge fan of his after seeing him star in HBO’s Game of Thrones so when she finally got a chance to meet him face-to-face, she didn’t even know how to react. Anyone meeting someone that they have a crush on in that way would probably react exactly the same! At least she was composed enough to get a picture with him.

9 Gal Gadot

Gal Gadot is a beautiful actress who plays the role of Wonder Woman in DC movies. She has worked alongside Jason Momoa in the live-action Justice League movie from 2017. In that movie, he played the role of Aquaman. Since the two have successfully worked together on the set of such an awesome heroic movie, they have a great friendship that goes beyond their acting careers.

8 Kelly Clarkson

When Jason Momoa appeared on Kelly Clarkson’s talk show, they had such a great conversation. They talked about everything under the sun that was interesting to them and their conversation totally flowed. Her kids even got involved in the mix and started asking him questions too! He is really good with kids which is why the interview ended up being a total success. His friendship with Kelly Clarkson, and her kids, is so sweet to see.

7 Amber Heard

Jason Momoa worked with Amber Heard in Aquaman where she played the character of Mira. Although Amber Heard has been going through legal issues of her own, battling it out against Johnny Depp in their super messy divorce, it doesn’t mean that she’s not able to still have Hollywood friends with whom she is still close. At the set of the movie, Jason would even prank her by stealing pages out of the books that she would be reading. It was all just fun and games!

6 Emilia Clarke

The friendship between Emilia Clarke and Jason Momoa blossomed on the set of HBO’s Game of Thrones where they played love interests. Obviously, anyone who has seen the show knows that her character was not keen on ending up with his character in marriage but it was forced upon her.

Then eventually, probably from Stockholm syndrome, her character ended up actually falling in love with the character he played! In real life, these two are friends who get along very well.

5 Julianne Nicholson

Jason Momoa is good friends with actress Julianne Nicholson. She is best known for being an Indie actress but she has done quite a lot to create a name for herself. They starred in a TV show called The Red Road together between 2014 and 2015 where they realized that they actually got along really well in real life! Meeting on the set of shows and movies is a great place for actors and actresses to create friendships.

4 Julia Roberts

When Julia Roberts and Jason Momoa took this super adorable selfie together, it pretty much let the whole world know how friendly they are with each other. He revealed that he has been a fan of Julia Roberts for a very long time so when he finally got to meet her, he was very excited about it. They got to take a selfie together which is really adorable. He ended up posting the selfies he took with her to his Instagram page to show the entire world.

3 Ellen DeGeneres

After Ellen DeGeneres interviewed Jason Momoa on her show, it became obvious that the two were going to be good friends. Ellen DeGeneres is known for creating a fun environment on her show with anyone who she invites to be interviewed.

She knows how to carry a fun conversation filled with a lot of humor. That is most likely why she and Jason Momoa got along so well while talking in front of the cameras.

2 Helen Mirren

Helen Mirren and Jason Momoa might seem like a random friendship but in reality, their friendship makes so much sense! As a silly joke, she admits that she one time took a secret photo of Jason Momoa on an airplane because she thought that he was one of the most gorgeous men she’d ever seen in her life! She isn’t the only woman in the world to feel so strongly about Jason Momoa and his rugged good looks.

1 Lisa Bonet

Of course, Jason Momoa’s life, and Lisa Bonet is going to be on this list. Jason Momoa and Lisa Bonet have been married since 2017 and they seem to be very happy together. He also gets along really well with her kids! He started crushing on her back in the 90s when she was filming Bill Cosby. It is so beautiful and amazing that they ended up together after he spent years finding her so attractive. They might be married but they are also best friends.
