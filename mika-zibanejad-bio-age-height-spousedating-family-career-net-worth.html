Mika Zibanejad Biography

Mika Zibanejad is a Swedish professional ice hockey centreman, playing for and serving as an alternate captain of the New York Rangers of the National Hockey League (NHL). He was selected sixth overall in the 2011 NHL Entry Draft by the Ottawa Senators and signed a professional contract with Ottawa in July 2011.

Mika Zibanejad Age|Nationality

He was born on 18 April 1993 and is therefore currently 26 years old as of 2019. Being born and raised in Huddinge, Sweden, Zibanejad holds the Swedish nationality and white ethnicity. His star|zodiac sign is Aries.
