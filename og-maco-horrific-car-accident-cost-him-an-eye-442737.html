OG Maco's Horrific Car Accident Cost Him An Eye

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 24, 2024

OG Maco was involved in a terrible car accident last week. The wreck resulted in several injuries, skull fractures, and a cracked vertebrate.

The Atlanta rapper has now spoken up to reveal the loss of an eye as well. He broke his orbital bone, resulting in a loss of sight from his right eye.

Although Maco has been placed through a few surgical procedures within the last week, he’s blessed to be able to live to see another day. He has taken his hospital accounts to Twitter, and won’t let this situation tear him down. He’s in good spirits.
